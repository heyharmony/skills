# Value Proposition Statements

> Turn your value proposition into ready-to-ship copy for marketing, sales, and onboarding.

## What It Does

Takes an existing value proposition and generates targeted statements for three contexts: marketing (headlines, taglines, body copy), sales (elevator pitches, objection-aware messaging, ROI statements), and onboarding (welcome messages, first-value prompts, milestone celebrations). When conversation data is available, mirrors the exact language your customers use.

## Who It's For

Product marketers, growth leads, and founders who have a value proposition but need it translated into copy that different teams can actually use — without hiring a copywriter for every channel.

## Use Cases

- Launching a campaign → get segment-specific headlines, subheadlines, and taglines ready for ads and landing pages
- Enabling the sales team → elevator pitches and objection-aware statements grounded in what prospects actually say
- Improving onboarding → welcome and milestone messages that reinforce why users signed up

## Setup

Install and go. Provide your existing value proposition (or product description and target audience). For copy that sounds like your customers, point to Harmony conversations — sales calls, interviews, or onboarding sessions.

## What You Get

A structured set of statements per segment: marketing (headline, subheadline, body, tagline), sales (elevator pitch, objection handler, ROI statement, competitive positioning), onboarding (welcome, first value, milestone), and a summary matrix.
