# Personalization at Scale

> Send 100+ personalized emails that feel 1:1, not blasted — using company intel, warm intros, and your own customer insights.

## What It Does

Generates truly personalized outreach for entire prospect lists in minutes instead of hours. Pull a CSV of 50 companies, and the skill creates a unique email for each one — referencing their recent funding, new hires in ops, shared connections, vertical-specific pain points, or all of the above. Uses your past conversation data with similar companies to inform talking points. Every email is different and feels human because it is — it's built from real research, not a template. Output is 100+ personalized emails (or multi-step sequences) ready to send, plus A/B test recommendations.

## Who It's For

- **Sales development reps** running outbound campaigns who want to personalize at scale, not spend 20 minutes per email
- **ABM teams** targeting 50+ accounts and wanting each prospect to feel special
- **SDRs launching a cold campaign** and wanting to beat benchmarks with personalization
- **AEs** doing account-based outreach and wanting to customize for each contact
- **Sales leaders** who want the team to personalize without manual effort burning them out

## Use Cases

- Launch new product feature to 50 prospects → 50 unique emails mentioning the feature and their specific situation
- Run ABM campaign on 30 accounts → Each account gets multiple personalized emails referencing company news, hiring, warm intros
- Segment outreach by industry → Logistics company gets logistics-specific talking points; healthcare gets healthcare-specific pain
- "Welcome to your new role" campaign → Every new ops hire in your target market gets a personalized email
- Batch prospecting week → Generate 100+ personalized emails Sunday night, send Monday morning as a team

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — Pulls contact and company data to auto-populate personalization variables; syncs outreach and tracks opens/clicks
- **Gmail** — Sends personalized emails directly and tracks delivery, opens, clicks
- **Slack** — Notifies team of high-intent replies so you can jump on them fast
- **Google Drive** — Stores prospect lists and personalization data; enables team sharing and versioning

## Setup

Start with a CSV or HubSpot export of prospects (company name, contact name, title, email). The skill will build personalized versions based on available data. Works best when you provide additional context (recent hiring, funding, warm intro paths), but generates output from company name + title if that's all you have. Connect Gmail to send directly from the skill output, and Slack to get notified of high-intent replies.

## What You Get

Personalized email templates (3–5 versions based on available prospect data), 100+ unique emails with names/companies/hooks customized for each prospect, A/B test recommendations showing which personalization hooks work best, and a prospect list summary showing which template was used for each person and why. Ready to send in batches immediately or import into your email tool.
