# GTM Motion Planner

> Stop doing everything badly. Score seven proven acquisition motions against your product, pick 2–4 that actually fit, and get a 90-day execution plan.

## What It Does

Evaluates seven go-to-market motions — Inbound, Outbound, Paid Digital, Community, Partners, ABM, and PLG — against your product, market, team, and budget. Scores each on fit, feasibility, speed, and scalability, then recommends a prioritized motion stack with specific tactics, tools, and timelines. When customer conversations exist in Harmony, it uses real discovery data to ground channel recommendations in how your customers actually find and evaluate solutions.

## Who It's For

- **Founders** choosing their first acquisition channels
- **Marketing leaders** rebalancing channel mix after a pivot or new funding
- **Growth teams** deciding where to double down or cut losses
- **GTM teams** aligning sales and marketing on a unified motion strategy

## Use Cases

- Series A with $15K/month budget, need to pick 2–3 channels → Scored motion stack with 90-day plan and budget allocation
- Enterprise product debating outbound vs ABM → Head-to-head comparison with specific tool and team recommendations
- PLG product not converting free to paid → Motion diagnosis and recommendations for what to add or change
- Post-pivot, all previous channels are wrong → Fresh motion scoring with new context and a phased restart plan

## Setup

Install and go. For the most grounded recommendations, have customer conversations in Harmony — the skill uses how customers describe discovering and evaluating solutions to validate channel fit.

## What You Get

A scored motion analysis with: context summary, 7-motion scorecard (fit, feasibility, speed, scalability), recommended primary + secondary + experimental motions with tactics and tools, motion interaction map, 90-day phased execution plan, metrics dashboard with monthly targets, and clear "skip for now" recommendations with reasoning.
