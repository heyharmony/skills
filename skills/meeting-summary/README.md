# Meeting Summary

> Never re-watch a meeting again — get decisions, action items, and key moments in a 60-second read.

## What It Does

Transforms any Harmony meeting transcript into a structured summary with decisions, action items (with owners and deadlines), key discussion points, and overall sentiment. Every insight links back to what was actually said — nothing gets lost in translation.

## Who It's For

Team leads and PMs in back-to-back meetings who need a fast, reliable way to capture and share what happened — without spending 20 minutes writing notes after the fact.

## Use Cases

- Sprint retro wraps up → structured recap with action items posted to a Harmony Note within seconds.
- Stakeholder missed a strategy meeting → scannable summary with decisions and open questions, all sourced from the transcript.
- Preparing for a follow-up call → pull up last meeting's summary to refresh context on what was decided and what's still open.

## Setup

Install and go. Works with any meeting recorded in Harmony — no additional configuration required.

## What You Get

A Markdown summary with: attendees, duration, decisions table, action items table (owners + due dates), key discussion points with speaker attribution, open questions, and a one-line sentiment read. Structured for forwarding, Slack, or consumption by other Harmony skills.
