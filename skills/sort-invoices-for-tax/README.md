# Sort Invoices for Tax

> **Organize invoices and receipts by tax category, flag missing documentation, identify deductions, and produce a tax-ready expense summary — all from uploaded files or finance meeting context.**

## What It Does

This skill transforms raw invoices and receipts into audit-ready tax reports. It:
- **Extracts** vendor, amount, date, and description from each expense
- **Categorizes** expenses by 10 standard tax buckets (SaaS, travel, professional services, contractors, etc.)
- **Flags** incomplete items, missing receipts, and ambiguous expenses
- **Identifies** overlooked deductions and tax credits (home office, mileage, Section 179, etc.)
- **Calculates** category totals, vendor rankings, quarterly breakdowns, and year-to-date summaries
- **Reconciles** against prior-year patterns and budget targets
- **Delivers** a structured, tax-ready expense report with action items

## Who It's For

- **Founders & small-business owners** organizing annual tax prep
- **Finance & accounting teams** reconciling multi-vendor expenses
- **Freelancers & consultants** preparing quarterly estimated tax filings
- **Finance leaders** performing year-end audit and compliance reviews
- **Teams with distributed expenses** across credit cards, contractors, and payment methods

## Use Cases

- **Q4 tax filing:** Organize 50+ Q4 invoices into categories, flag missing receipts, prepare for CPA review
- **Quarterly expense checks:** Validate that Q3 spend aligns with budget; surface unexpected categories
- **Multi-vendor reconciliation:** Consolidate 20+ vendor invoices from email, Stripe, and Google Drive into one report
- **Missing receipt recovery:** Flag which vendors need to reissue receipts; request confirmations
- **Deduction discovery:** Identify Section 179 equipment purchases, home office eligibility, mileage tracking gaps
- **Team reimbursement:** Categorize and validate contractor payments for 1099 reporting
- **Travel expense audit:** Organize international and domestic travel by trip; validate meal and lodging caps

## Setup

1. **Authorize connectors** (optional): Link Google Drive, Gmail, Stripe, and Notion for automated data pull
2. **Upload files** or reference existing folder paths to expense data
3. **Provide context** (optional): Share relevant finance meeting notes, budget spreadsheets, or accounting instructions
4. **Run the skill** and review the generated tax-ready report
