# Brand Guidelines

> Apply your brand consistently across every artifact your team creates.

## What It Does

Brand Guidelines helps you translate your company's visual identity and voice into every presentation, document, report, and dashboard you create. It covers color palettes, typography, logo usage, spacing rules, and tone of voice. If you don't have formal guidelines yet, it also walks you through defining a brand identity from scratch—starting from your positioning and audience.

You end with a reusable brand toolkit and a checklist for applying brand consistently to any artifact.

## Who It's For

- **Design and Creative Teams:** Building and maintaining brand consistency across all company artifacts
- **Product and Marketing Teams:** Creating presentations, reports, and customer-facing documents that feel professional and on-brand
- **Operations and Communications:** Ensuring all internal updates, announcements, and templates match company visual identity
- **Startup Founders and Solopreneurs:** Defining brand guidelines from scratch and applying them across proposals, decks, and customer work
- **Engineering and Data Teams:** Styling dashboards, reports, and technical documentation with consistent design
- **Anyone Creating Company Artifacts:** Presentations, documents, templates, graphics, emails, anything representing your company

## Use Cases

- **Create a Brand Toolkit:** Start from scratch by defining colors, fonts, voice, and logo rules. Document it so everyone can use it.
- **Apply Brand to a Deck:** Turn a plain presentation into a branded, visually cohesive deck that lands with investors or clients.
- **Build a Template Library:** Create reusable templates for monthly updates, project charters, and reports so brand is baked in.
- **Audit for Consistency:** Compare your recent artifacts (decks, reports, emails) and identify inconsistencies; get guidance on fixing them.
- **Refresh an Aging Brand:** Your brand has evolved; apply updated colors, fonts, and voice to recent work to keep it fresh.
- **Style a Dashboard or Report:** Apply color coding, typography hierarchy, and spacing rules so data is both beautiful and readable.

## Setup

Install the skill. To apply brand to an artifact or define guidelines:
1. Gather your brand elements (colors, fonts, logo, voice) or positioning if starting from scratch
2. Trigger with *"apply brand to this"* or *"define our brand guidelines"*
3. Choose your artifact type (presentation, document, report, etc.)
4. Work through the steps; the skill provides templates and checklists
5. End with branded artifacts and a reusable brand toolkit for your team

## What You Get

A consistent, recognizable brand identity applied across all your company work. You'll have a reusable brand toolkit (colors, fonts, voice) that you can share with your team, templates for recurring artifacts, and a checklist to ensure consistency. The skill also suggests what's next—whether that's updating your wiki with brand guidelines, creating templates, or auditing other artifacts for consistency.
