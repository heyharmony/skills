# Navigate Difficult Conversations

> Deliver hard feedback, address conflict, and conduct crucial conversations with care and clarity that builds trust, not distance.

## What It Does

This skill prepares you for and guides you through difficult conversations—critical feedback, performance issues, conflicts, and terminations. It combines Radical Candor (care personally + challenge directly) with structured frameworks like SBI (Situation-Behavior-Impact) to help you be honest and human at the same time.

Includes prep templates, question guides, emotional safety considerations, and post-conversation documentation to ensure clarity and follow-up.

## Who It's For

- Managers giving critical feedback or addressing performance issues
- Leaders navigating conflict between team members or peers
- Anyone preparing for a termination or role change conversation
- Teams wanting to address issues early (pinches before crunches)
- First-time managers learning how to deliver hard truths with care
- People managing up—giving feedback to your manager or peers
- Anyone trying to improve relationship quality while being direct

## Use Cases

- Preparing critical feedback that might feel harsh but needs to land clearly
- Addressing performance issues with specific expectations and timeline for improvement
- Navigating difficult conversations about compensation, role fit, or transition
- Handling employee conflicts or behavior concerns with fairness
- Conducting terminations with dignity, clear logistics, and humanity
- Addressing tension before it escalates to crisis (early pinches)
- Delivering feedback to peers or your manager in a way that builds relationship
- Managing conflict between team members by staying neutral and solution-focused
- Preparing for conversations you've been avoiding, with structure and confidence

## Connectors

- **Slack** – Provides context on recent incidents, patterns, or communication
- **Google Calendar** – Helps schedule adequate time and private space
- **Notion** – Stores conversation notes, warnings, and documentation for continuity

## Setup

1. Connect Slack to surface context on incidents or patterns
2. Link Google Calendar for scheduling private time for sensitive conversations
3. Set up Notion workspace for conversation documentation (optional)
4. Use the skill before any conversation you're dreading or uncertain about

## What You Get

- Pre-conversation prep that combines care with clarity
- SBI framework (Situation-Behavior-Impact) to keep feedback factual
- Emotional safety guidance to help people hear hard truths
- Question guide to understand their perspective before assuming
- Performance expectations and improvement plans when needed
- Termination conversation structure with dignity and logistics
- Post-conversation summary to document and follow up within 24 hours
