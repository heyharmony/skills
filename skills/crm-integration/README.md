# CRM Auto-Updater

> Every conversation updates your CRM automatically — no manual note-taking, no stale data.

## What It Does

Pulls insights from Harmony conversations (sales calls, customer meetings, discovery sessions) and syncs them into your CRM (HubSpot or Salesforce) automatically. Extracts what was discussed, action items, next steps, buying signals, and updates deal records, contacts, and activity feeds. Reps stay focused on selling, not data entry. CRM stays current with conversation context without manual logging.

## Who It's For

- **Account Executives** — Spend time closing deals, not updating Salesforce; every call auto-syncs
- **Sales Development Managers** — Track team activity and next steps without asking for manual notes
- **Sales leaders** — Deal records reflect latest customer feedback; forecast based on real conversation data
- **RevOps teams** — CRM data quality improves automatically; less manual audits needed
- **Customer Success managers** — Customer interactions and action items automatically logged for handoff

## Use Cases

- After a sales call → CRM deal record auto-updates with summary, next step, and timeline
- Discovery meeting shows new stakeholder → Contact auto-created in CRM with role and decision authority
- Customer mentioned budget during call → Budget field auto-updated in deal record
- Negotiation call surfaces concerns → Risk assessment auto-logged in deal notes
- Weekly deal review → All CRM records reflect latest conversation context from Harmony

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot** — Automatically sync call summaries, next steps, and deal stage recommendations into deal and contact records
- **Salesforce** — Push Harmony insights (call notes, action items, buying signals) into Salesforce opportunities and accounts
- **Slack** — Alert sales team when CRM data has been updated from a Harmony conversation

## Setup

Install and go. Connect HubSpot or Salesforce so the skill can push updates. After each conversation in Harmony (recorded call, transcript, or meeting notes), the skill extracts key data and syncs to the right CRM record.

## What You Get

CRM records stay current with conversation context: deal summaries, next steps, action items, contact information (names, titles, roles in deal), buying signals, timeline signals, and risk assessment — all pulled from actual Harmony conversations and automatically synced so reps don't have to manually log notes.
