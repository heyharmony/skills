# GTM Strategy Builder

> Turn customer conversations and market research into a launch-ready go-to-market plan — channels, messaging, metrics, and timeline in one structured document.

## What It Does

Builds a complete go-to-market strategy from whatever you've got: discovery calls, customer interviews, competitive research, or just a product idea and a target market. When your conversations live in Harmony, the strategy pulls real customer language, objections, and buying signals directly from transcripts — so your positioning is grounded in what people actually said, not what you think they meant.

## Who It's For

- **Product managers** launching a new product or feature
- **Founders** going to market for the first time or entering a new segment
- **Marketing leads** building channel strategy and messaging frameworks
- **GTM teams** aligning on positioning, metrics, and launch timelines

## Use Cases

- Discovery calls done, need to turn insights into a launch plan → Full GTM strategy with voice-of-customer evidence
- New market entry with no existing data → Structured strategy with validation recommendations and learn-launch framework
- Repositioning after a pivot → Updated positioning, messaging, and channel mix grounded in recent customer feedback
- Leadership wants a launch brief by Friday → Executive-ready GTM document with timeline, metrics, and risks

## Setup

Install and go. For strongest results, have customer conversations or discovery call recordings in Harmony — the skill will pull real quotes and objection patterns to ground your strategy.

## What You Get

A structured GTM strategy document covering: market positioning, voice-of-customer evidence, prioritized channel strategy, audience-specific messaging with objection handling, success metrics by launch phase, phased timeline, and risk mitigations.
