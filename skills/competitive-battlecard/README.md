# Competitive Battlecard Builder

> Give your sales team the exact words to win against any competitor — grounded in what prospects actually say on calls, not marketing guesswork.

## What It Does

Builds a scannable, sales-ready battlecard for any competitor by combining live web research with your own conversation data. When sales calls in Harmony mention the competitor, the battlecard includes real prospect objections, the exact language buyers use when comparing, and win/loss patterns from your own deals — not generic competitive theory.

## Who It's For

- **Sales leaders** enabling their team to handle competitive deals
- **Product marketers** building competitive positioning and enablement materials
- **AEs and SDRs** preparing for calls where a specific competitor is in play
- **Founders** who keep hearing the same competitor name and need a structured response

## Use Cases

- Prospect just said "we're also looking at [Competitor X]" → Battlecard with head-to-head comparison, objection responses, and landmine questions within minutes
- Quarterly competitive refresh → Updated battlecard pulling latest web intel and recent competitive mentions from calls
- Lost three deals to the same competitor → Win/loss pattern analysis with specific counter-positioning recommendations
- New competitor entered the market → Research-driven battlecard with initial positioning and monitoring recommendations

## Setup

Install and go. For the strongest battlecards, have sales calls in Harmony where prospects mention competitors — the skill extracts real objections, comparison language, and win/loss signals directly from your conversations.

## What You Get

A structured battlecard with: competitor snapshot, head-to-head feature comparison, where-we-win evidence, where-they-win counter-positioning, top objection responses with proof points, landmine questions to plant early, win/loss patterns, trap plays for specific scenarios, and a quick-reference section for mid-call use.
