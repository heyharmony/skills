# Social Content

> Turn your expertise and conversations into social content that builds audience and drives business.

## What It Does

Creates social media content across LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook — individual posts, threads, carousels, content calendars, and repurposing plans. Includes hook formulas, content pillar frameworks, engagement strategy, and scheduling templates. When conversation data is available, turns real meeting insights and customer stories into authentic, high-performing posts.

## Who It's For

Founders, marketers, and thought leaders who want to build a consistent social presence without spending all day on it — whether creating from scratch or repurposing existing content across platforms.

## Use Cases

- Building a weekly content calendar → get 5 LinkedIn posts, 3 Twitter threads, and Instagram content planned and written in one sitting
- Repurposing a blog post or talk → content adapted to each platform's format and norms, not just cross-posted
- Turning meeting insights into posts → anonymized, authentic content from real Harmony conversations that resonates because it's grounded in reality

## Setup

Install and go. Provide your goals, audience, platforms, and brand voice. For a content strategy, share your content pillars or let the skill suggest them. For repurposing, provide source content. For conversation-driven posts, point to Harmony recordings of meetings, talks, or customer calls.

## What You Get

Platform-specific posts with hooks, body copy, CTAs, and reasoning. Optionally a content calendar, content pillar strategy, repurposing plan, and engagement routine.
