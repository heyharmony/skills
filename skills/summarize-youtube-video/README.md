# Summarize YouTube Video

> Turn a 45-minute talk into a 2-minute read with timestamps and action items.

## What It Does

Paste a YouTube URL, and get back a structured summary with key takeaways, timestamped highlights, notable quotes, and action items. The skill fetches the video transcript, identifies critical segments, and formats everything so you can skim in 2 minutes or jump to specific moments in the video. No need to watch the whole thing if you don't have time.

## Setup

Install and go. Provide a YouTube URL with captions (manual or auto-generated). The skill fetches the transcript, cleans duplicates from auto-generated captions, and processes the content into a structured summary. Works with talks, tutorials, webinars, interviews, and conference presentations. When Harmony conversation data exists — such as a meeting where someone recommended a video — the skill connects extracted insights to the discussion context.

## What You Get

A structured summary that opens with a watch recommendation (full watch, skim, or skip) and speaker context so you know who's talking and why they're credible. Key takeaways are listed in priority order with supporting evidence from the talk. A timestamped highlights section lets you jump to specific moments — each with a one-line summary of what's said. Notable quotes are pulled verbatim with timestamps so you can reference or share the most impactful lines.

The summary closes with action items that translate the video's advice into concrete next steps for your team. If the video references frameworks, tools, or resources, those are cataloged separately for easy follow-up. Everything follows a consistent format, so your video summary library stays organized whether you're processing a 10-minute tutorial or a 90-minute conference keynote.

## Tips for Best Results

Videos with manual (human-created) subtitles produce the cleanest summaries. Auto-generated captions work well but may contain minor transcription errors — the skill deduplicates and cleans these automatically. For long videos (60+ minutes), specify which topics you care about so the summary prioritizes relevant segments. Panel discussions with multiple speakers get speaker attribution when the transcript includes speaker labels.
