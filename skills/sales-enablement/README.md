# Build Sales Enablement Assets

## Overview
Sales closes more deals with collateral they trust. This skill creates battle-tested sales materials grounded in real conversation data from your Harmony workspace.

Extract exact objections, winning language, and competitive mentions from your pipeline calls, then transform them into sales decks (10-12 slide story arcs), one-pagers (30-second scans), objection handling docs, demo scripts, and competitive battlecards.

## What It Does
- **Mines conversation data** from HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Gmail, and Google Drive to surface real objections, competitive threats, buyer language, and proof points
- **Builds five asset types:** sales decks, one-pagers, objection handlers, demo scripts, competitive battlecards
- **Grounds every asset** in actual deal moments—no generic corporate templates
- **Outputs structured collateral** ready to distribute to your sales team

## When to Use
- Creating or refreshing a sales deck with a proven story arc
- Building a competitive battlecard against a known competitor
- Scripting demo walkthroughs customized by persona
- Developing objection handling docs tied to real deal blockers
- Fixing low adoption by regenerating materials in language reps actually use
- Capturing post-sale validation from customer success calls

## Key Principle
**Sales Uses What Sales Trusts.** Materials must involve actual reps and reflect real buyer conversation. This skill extracts language from your data, not a playbook.

## Connectors
- HubSpot (deal data, win/loss insights, competitor mentions)
- Salesforce (opportunity records, competitive intelligence)
- Slack (rep feedback, deal discussions, competitive intel channels)
- Gmail (prospect objections, customer email threads)
- Google Drive (existing sales templates, brand guidelines)

## Output
A structured asset file containing:
- Metadata (asset type, persona, creation date, conversation sources)
- Content blocks tailored to asset type (slides, one-pager sections, objection rebuttals, demo script flow, battlecard matrices)
- Proof points grounded in specific conversations
- Rep guidance on when and how to deploy

## Example Assets
- **Sales Deck:** 10-12 slide problem → cost → shift → approach → proof → case study → implementation → ROI → pricing → next steps
- **One-Pager:** Problem | Solution | Differentiators | Proof | CTA (30-second scan)
- **Objection Doc:** Price | Timing | Competition | Authority | Status Quo | Technical—each with proven rebuttals
- **Demo Script:** Opening hook → discovery questions → solution walkthrough → close
- **Battlecard:** Competitive positioning | Feature matrix | Proof points | Counter-messaging

## How It Works
1. Gather recent sales calls, deal notes, email threads
2. Specify collateral type and buyer persona
3. Extract winning language, objections, and competitive mentions
4. Map insights to your asset structure
5. Draft in rep language and validate with field team
6. Iterate and deploy

Run this skill once per quarter per asset to keep materials fresh and battle-tested.
