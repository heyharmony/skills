# Cold Email Sequence Generator

> Multi-touch campaigns that rotate angles so each follow-up feels like a different person reaching out with new information — not a machine on repeat.

## What It Does

Builds a complete 4-5 email cold email sequence with rotating angles, proof points, and resources. Each email in the sequence adds something new — a different proof point, a useful resource, a company signal — so the prospect gets a reason to engage on the second, third, or fourth touch instead of perceiving spam. Generates subject lines, body copy, and timing recommendations for each email. When conversation data is available, grounds angles and proof points in what actually resonates from successful sales calls.

## Who It's For

- **Sales leaders and SDRs** building outbound campaigns for new personas
- **RevOps teams** creating repeatable email workflows across sales segments
- **Growth teams** scaling outbound sales and testing angle rotation
- **Founders** doing their own prospecting and needing email sequences that actually work

## Use Cases

- Building outbound for VP Sales at Series B–C SaaS → 5-email sequence with problem angle, metric proof, case study, hiring trigger, and breakup email
- Scaling SDR prospecting to a new buyer persona → Adapted sequence for a different role with personalized openers and proofs
- Improving reply rates on existing campaigns → Refresh angles and proof points, test new subject lines, adjust timing
- Testing which angle wins → Run two sequences side-by-side with different angle rotation to see which converts better

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Gmail** — Access past templates and subject lines that worked; ground new sequences in what your team has already validated
- **HubSpot** — Set up sequences as automated workflows and track reply rate, open rate, and CTC by angle and persona
- **Slack** — Get weekly summaries of sequence performance so the team knows which angles are winning
- **Linear** — Create tasks to A/B test subject lines and document learnings for next iteration

## Setup

Install and go. Provide who you're reaching (role, persona, segment), what your value prop is, what proof you have, and what you want them to do. The skill generates the full sequence with timing and reasoning.

## What You Get

A complete multi-touch email sequence with subject lines, personalized body copy, specific timing between touches, reasoning for each angle, performance tips, and a/b testing recommendations.
