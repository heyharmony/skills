# Voice Call Intelligence

> Turn every phone call into a coaching moment and a close signal—sentiment shifts, objection handling, and sales signals are just as visible in transcripts as they are in body language.

## What It Does

Phone calls lack the visual context of video meetings, but Harmony transcribes and analyzes them the same way. This skill extracts sentiment trajectories, objection handling effectiveness, and hidden buying signals from voice-only conversations—cold calls, support escalations, internal syncs—and maps them to the same intelligence layer as video meetings. You get a structured debrief that shows what the prospect was thinking, where your pitch worked, where it stumbled, and what to do next.

## Who It's For

- **Sales Development Reps** — Cold calls and first conversations. You need to know: Did I sound confident? Did they seem interested? What objections should I prepare for next time?
- **Account Executives** — Demo calls and negotiations. You need to know: Where did sentiment shift? Did I handle that Competitor X objection well? What's the real buying signal underneath their words?
- **Sales Managers** — Coaching and rep development. You need to know: Which reps are good at objection handling? Who needs coaching on discovery? What patterns do I see across my top deals?
- **Support Leaders** — Escalations and retention. You need to know: What causes customers to escalate? Which resolution patterns actually stick? How's my team's tone landing?
- **Customer Success Managers** — Renewal and expansion calls. You need to know: Is our customer satisfied or just quiet? Where are the expansion hooks? What's their real sentiment?

## Use Cases

- **Cold call debrief** — You hit a tough objection on 10 calls this week. Which reps handled it well? What did they say? Use this to coach your team on a real pattern.
- **Support escalation analysis** — A customer blew up on a call. Analyze sentiment trajectory to find the moment they lost patience. Train your team on de-escalation.
- **Demo follow-up coaching** — Your AE finished a demo call. Extract sentiment arc, objection handling, and next steps so you can coach before the next interaction.
- **Cold call effectiveness scoring** — New SDR's first 10 calls. Score each one on sentiment, discovery, objection handling. Use the patterns to improve their approach.
- **Pricing objection patterns** — Across your last 20 calls, how often does price come up? How well is it handled? What patterns do you see?

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — Syncs voice call insights directly to deal records. Flag sentiment changes, objection patterns, and next-step commitments in your CRM.
- **Help Scout** — Routes support call intelligence to your support team. Extract escalation triggers, CSAT drivers, and coaching moments from customer support conversations.
- **Slack** — Posts coaching alerts when strong moments or rough moments are detected. Surface patterns to your sales team in real time.
- **Google Calendar** — Maps call frequency and attendee escalation to surface relationship momentum changes over time.
- **Linear** — Creates follow-up tasks from voice call insights: prospect demos, objection research, coaching actions.

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your phone calls are transcribed in Harmony. Without transcripts, describe the call and the skill works from your notes.

## What You Get

A complete voice call debrief: sentiment trajectory from opening to closing, objection handling scorecard with coaching notes, discovery intelligence extracted, what went well and what could improve, commitments from both sides, next steps and timing, and a call quality score (0–100) that separates rep performance from deal outcome. Ends with specific coaching recommendations and next-step actions.
