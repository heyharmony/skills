# Cold Email

> Cold emails that sound human, land in the primary inbox, and actually get replies.

## What It Does

Writes B2B cold emails and multi-touch follow-up sequences — subject lines, personalization, body copy, and CTAs. Each email is calibrated to the prospect's role and seniority, uses a low-friction ask, and follows a structure that connects personalization to the problem. When conversation data is available, grounds messaging in the phrases and proof points that actually resonate in successful sales conversations.

## Who It's For

Sales teams, SDRs/BDRs, founders doing their own outbound, and growth teams who need cold outreach that doesn't sound like every other email in the prospect's inbox.

## Use Cases

- Writing cold outreach for a new ICP → get a personalized email plus 4-email follow-up sequence with angle rotation
- Improving reply rates → rewrite existing templates to sound human, lead with the prospect's world, and use low-friction CTAs
- Scaling outbound after successful sales calls → extract winning phrases and proof points from Harmony conversations into repeatable email patterns

## Setup

Install and go. Provide who you're writing to, what you want (meeting, reply, intro), your value prop, and any proof points. For emails grounded in real sales patterns, point to Harmony conversations (successful discovery calls, closed-won deal recordings).

## What You Get

One or more cold emails with subject lines, full body copy, and reasoning. Optionally a 3-5 email follow-up sequence with cadence, angle rotation, and a breakup email. Plus alternative subject lines and quality notes on what would make the emails stronger.
