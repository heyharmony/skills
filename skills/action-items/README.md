# Action Item Extractor

> Turn any meeting into a clean, assignable task list — with owners, deadlines, priorities, and the context your team needs to execute.

## What It Does

Extracts every action item from a specific meeting and organizes them by priority, owner, and deadline. Goes beyond explicit "action item" callouts to catch implicit commitments — the "I'll look into it" moments, the questions that need answers, and the decisions that require follow-up implementation. Each item includes enough context that anyone reading the list can execute without re-watching the meeting.

## Who It's For

- **Product managers** sending follow-up emails after planning meetings
- **Team leads** making sure nothing from standups and syncs gets lost
- **Project managers** updating trackers after cross-functional meetings
- **Anyone** who attends meetings and needs a clear list of what happens next

## Use Cases

- Sprint planning just wrapped, 55 minutes of discussion → 9 prioritized action items with owners, deadlines, and dependencies mapped
- Customer call ended with several follow-ups → Action items split by "ours" vs. "theirs" with context for each
- You missed a meeting and need to know what was decided → Full action item list with decisions made and your items highlighted
- Chaotic brainstorm with lots of "let's do this" energy → Structured list separating real commitments from ideas, flagging unassigned items

## Setup

Install and go. Works best with meetings recorded in Harmony — the skill scans transcripts for commitment language, ownership signals, and deadline mentions. For meetings without transcripts, describe what was discussed and the skill will help you formalize action items.

## What You Get

Action items organized by priority (high/medium/low) with owner, deadline, and context for each. Unassigned items flagged separately so they don't get dropped. Key decisions documented for context. Dependencies between items mapped. Summary with your personal items highlighted and total counts. Option to create tasks in Linear, draft a follow-up email, or check against existing tracked items.
