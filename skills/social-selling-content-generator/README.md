# Social Selling Content Generator

> Turn what your customers actually say — their objections, wins, insights — into authentic LinkedIn content that resonates and drives inbound leads.

## What It Does

Extracts real customer quotes, objections, and insights from sales calls and customer conversations, then transforms them into LinkedIn posts, articles, and social content that establish you (or your team) as credible voices in your market. Instead of generic "tips for closing deals," you're sharing specific patterns you're seeing, competitive dynamics you're navigating, and real buyer perspectives — because you have the evidence from actual conversations. Every post is grounded in something that happened on a call, making it more believable and shareable than abstract advice.

## Who It's For

- **Sales reps** building a personal brand on LinkedIn and needing authentic talking points from real customer conversations
- **Sales leaders** running social selling campaigns and needing 20+ posts to hit different themes (objections, use cases, competitive positioning)
- **Product marketers** wanting to create thought leadership content grounded in actual buyer feedback, not assumptions
- **Sales organizations** trying to standardize social selling across the team and wanting a content library reps can use

## Use Cases

- "Competitor is losing on implementation speed. I have 5 customer calls saying exactly this. Create 10 posts I can publish this month" → Content pack focusing on setup speed, ease, competitive differentiation
- Weekly social selling cadence → Generate 2–3 posts every week from last week's customer calls (objections heard, wins closed, patterns observed)
- Sales rep personal brand launch → Pull insights from top-performer calls; create 20-post starter pack they can publish over 2 months
- Campaign launch support → Create 15 posts that reinforce the campaign theme (e.g., "Why mid-market struggles with integrations") grounded in recent customer calls

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Harmony conversations** — Analyzes sales calls and customer meetings; extracts exact quotes, objection patterns, and competitive insights that become compelling social content
- **Salesforce / HubSpot** — Pulls win data, deal outcomes, and metrics to prove claims with specifics (e.g., "70% of our customers hit ROI in X weeks")
- **Slack** — Posts content drafts to team channels for quick feedback and approval before publishing; tracks engagement on shared posts
- **Google Calendar** — Maps customer calls to ensure content themes align with seasonal focus or campaign timing

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your sales calls live in Harmony — the skill will listen to actual calls and extract real quotes that make social content stand out. Without that, it will generate content from customer stories and competitive context you provide, but the authenticity of actual call quotes is the differentiator.

## What You Get

A complete social selling content pack ready to publish: 5–20 posts depending on scope, each with platform-specific formatting (LinkedIn post, article, Twitter), hooks designed for engagement, body copy grounded in customer conversations, and CTAs that drive action. Includes performance expectations (typical reach and engagement for a sales rep or company account) and iteration guidance — which themes and formats work best with your audience.
