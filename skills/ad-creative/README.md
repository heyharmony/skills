# Generate Ad Creative

Turn every conversation into proven ad copy. Extract the exact language prospects use when describing their problems and desired outcomes — the fastest path to high-converting ads.

## Overview

This skill bridges the gap between what your team *thinks* resonates with customers and what *actually* does. Every customer call, support email, and sales conversation contains gold: specific language, objections, pain points, and desired outcomes. This skill surfaces that language and transforms it into platform-specific ad copy.

## What It Does

- **Extracts prospect language** from calls, emails, and Slack threads to ground ad copy in real customer voice
- **Generates platform-specific copy** for Google Ads (RSAs), Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Twitter — respecting character limits and audience norms
- **Develops multiple angles** (pain point, outcome, social proof, curiosity, objection flip) to test messaging hypotheses fast
- **Applies quality standards**: specificity over vagueness, active voice, benefit-first framing, and measurable proof points
- **Iterates based on data**: When ads run, identifies underperformers and rewrites using new call insights

## When to Use This Skill

- You're launching a new campaign and copy feels generic
- Your ads have plateaued and need new angles tested
- You need Google Ads RSA headlines, Meta long-form, or platform-specific copy fast
- You want to test 6+ ad variations simultaneously
- Your analytics show which ad segments underperform; you want to rewrite them
- You're onboarding new team members and need consistent messaging

## Quick Start

1. Paste a recent customer call transcript, email thread, or support chat
2. Specify your target platform(s)
3. Pick your angle: pain point, outcome, social proof, or curiosity
4. Get back platform-ready copy in minutes

No transcript? No problem. Skill works with positioning docs and market research too — but real customer language is always stronger.

## Key Features

**Platform Expertise:** Knows Google Ads RSA headline limits (30 chars), Meta primary text hooks (first 125 chars visible), LinkedIn professional norms (150 chars), and TikTok conversational tone

**Angle Development:** Choose from pain point (highlight the broken), outcome (emphasize improvement), social proof (show results), curiosity (tease a gap), or objection flip (address hesitation directly)

**Quality Standards:** Specificity beats vagueness. "Cut audit time from 3 weeks to 1 day" converts better than "Faster audits." Active voice, benefit-first framing, and numbers always included

**Data-Driven Iteration:** Once ads run, use HubSpot conversion data and new customer language patterns to refine copy and test new angles

## Connectors Used

- **HubSpot**: Track conversion rates by ad variant and lead quality
- **Slack**: Share team feedback and competitive ad examples
- **Gmail**: Extract customer language from email threads
- **Google Drive**: Access brand guidelines and performance reports

## Output Examples

**Google Ads RSA Headline (30 chars max):**
Cut audit weeks into days

**Meta Primary Text (first 125 chars hook):**
Compliance audits took 3 weeks. Now? 2 days. Vaultkey automates the entire workflow...

**LinkedIn Ad (150 chars, professional):**
Compliance audits took 3 weeks. Now? 2 days. See how Vaultkey cut audit prep time by 93%.

## Tips for Best Results

- Ground copy in real customer language when possible (calls, emails, support tickets)
- Test multiple angles simultaneously: one pain-point variant, one outcome variant, one social proof variant
- Keep brand voice consistent across platforms while adapting to platform norms (LinkedIn stays formal; TikTok goes conversational)
- Always include specificity and proof: numbers, customer count, time saved, or outcome achieved
- Iterate: When ads run, check which angle resonates most and rewrite underperformers
