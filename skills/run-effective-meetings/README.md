# Run Effective Meetings

> Transform meetings from status theater into decision-making and alignment engines where clarity wins and action follows.

## What It Does

This skill helps you design and execute meetings that actually move work forward. It separates strategic decisions from operational updates, pushes status to async channels, and ensures every meeting ends with crystal-clear decisions, action items, and communication plans.

Includes meeting design templates, pre-read structure, facilitation guidance, and post-meeting summaries that leave no ambiguity about who's doing what.

## Who It's For

- Leaders running planning, alignment, and decision-making meetings
- Teams trying to cut meeting bloat without losing coordination or efficiency
- Anyone who senses meetings are spinning wheels without landing decisions
- Facilitators of cross-functional projects, retrospectives, and all-hands
- Individual contributors preparing for important meetings with leadership
- Teams transitioning to async-first or distributed work environments

## Use Cases

- Planning quarterly roadmap decisions with trade-off clarity and stakeholder alignment
- Running all-hands meetings that land key messages and align teams
- Designing decision-making meetings with proper prep, debate, and follow-up
- Leading retrospectives that produce real action items and continuous improvement
- Converting recurring meetings from status theater into strategic forums
- Preparing for important cross-functional alignment or kickoff meetings
- Facilitating brainstorms that generate ideas without devolving into chaos
- Managing time in highly synchronous organizations to reduce meeting overload

## Connectors

- **Google Calendar** – Syncs attendees, prior meeting patterns, and scheduling context
- **Gmail** – Surfaces email threads and relevant background communication
- **Slack** – Captures ongoing decisions and discussion threads to avoid redundancy
- **Notion** – Stores meeting templates, decision logs, and follow-up tracking

## Setup

1. Connect your calendar and Slack workspace for full context
2. Add Gmail connection to pull relevant email background (optional)
3. Create a Notion database for decision logging (optional but recommended)
4. Use the skill before planning any important meeting or when you feel meetings are ineffective

## What You Get

- Meeting design template tailored to meeting type (decision, alignment, brainstorm, retrospective, all-hands)
- Pre-read structure to move information transfer async and ensure people come prepared with perspective
- Facilitation guidance for staying on track and managing group dynamics (dominators, quiet voices, scope creep)
- Three-question ending protocol (What decided? Who's doing what? Who needs to know?) to ensure clarity
- Post-meeting summary to drive action and close the loop within 24 hours with clear ownership
- Decision log for continuity and accountability across meetings
- Framework for knowing when to cancel a meeting vs. when it's truly necessary
- Guidance for managing time zones and async participation in distributed teams
