# Page CRO Analyzer

Optimize marketing pages for higher conversion rates by auditing value proposition, headlines, CTAs, visual hierarchy, trust signals, objection handling, and friction points.

## What It Does

This skill performs a structured audit of any marketing page (landing page, pricing page, product page, feature page) to identify why visitors aren't converting. It uses Harmony conversation data—customer calls, Slack discussions, email threads—to ground recommendations in real prospect language, objections, and decision drivers.

The analysis framework is impact-ordered: it prioritizes fixes that move the conversion needle most (value prop, headline, CTA placement) before addressing secondary factors (visual hierarchy, trust signals, friction reduction).

## When to Use

- Your page gets traffic but bounce rate is high or conversion rate is stuck.
- You're redesigning and want validation that the new version will convert better.
- Prospects mention competitor alternatives in calls; need stronger differentiation on page.
- Form abandonment is spiking; need to identify and fix friction.
- You're launching a new product or feature and need messaging that resonates.

## How It Works

1. Share the page (URL, screenshot, or content).
2. I pull your customer voice from Harmony—exact language prospects use, objections they raise, how they talk about your solution.
3. I audit value prop, headline, CTA, visual hierarchy, trust signals, objection-handling, and friction.
4. I recommend fixes in priority order, with A/B test ideas to validate impact.

## Key Connectors

- **HubSpot** — Landing page metrics, form completion rates, lead source data.
- **Slack** — Team discussions about page performance, customer feedback, competitive mentions.
- **Gmail** — Customer email threads revealing objections, language patterns, deal blockers.
- **Google Drive** — Wireframes, design docs, competitor screenshots for context.

## Example Triggers

- "Can you audit our pricing page for conversion optimization?"
- "Why isn't this landing page converting better? We're getting traffic but no leads."
- "What A/B tests would you recommend for this page?"
- "I see customers mention our competitor in calls. How do I make sure our page stands out?"
