# Deal Momentum Analyzer

> Turn intuition into numbers — score every deal's momentum and see which ones are really accelerating toward close and which are stalling without you realizing it.

## What It Does

Analyzes all the meetings and interactions on a deal to score momentum: are things accelerating (more meetings, more stakeholders, clearer decision path), staying steady (normal cadence, consistent engagement), or declining (fewer meetings, same people talking, vague timeline)? Uses call frequency, stakeholder involvement, buyer sentiment from transcripts, and commitment signals (stated decision dates, budget clarity) to quantify what your gut probably already knows. Ends with a specific momentum score and intervention strategy if the deal is losing steam.

## Who It's For

- **Sales reps** who have a gut feel that a deal is losing momentum but can't prove it (now you can)
- **Sales managers** doing pipeline reviews and needing to separate "on track" from "stalling but looks okay on paper"
- **Forecast accuracy owners** trying to predict which forecast commits will actually close
- **Deal coaches** helping reps understand the difference between a meeting and real progress

## Use Cases

- Weekly deal check-in → "This deal scored 35/100 momentum. It's declining. Here's what to do this week to recover it."
- Pipeline review → Identify 3–4 deals that are showing stable momentum (green), 2–3 showing decline (yellow), and any at critical risk (red)
- Rep coaching → "You've had 3 meetings but no new stakeholders. That's a risk signal. Here's how to expand the buying committee."
- Forecast accuracy → Track which momentum scores actually convert; use that to calibrate your forecast confidence threshold
- Dealing with "this feels stuck" → Quantify the feeling with data; prove to the customer/manager that momentum is declining and action is needed

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Harmony meetings** — Analyzes all customer calls on the deal to assess engagement depth, stakeholder involvement, and buying signal strength; pulls sentiment and decision timeline clarity
- **Google Calendar** — Maps meeting frequency and gaps to identify cadence trends; shows when momentum might be slowing (longer gaps between meetings)
- **Salesforce / HubSpot** — Cross-references meeting activity with deal stage and forecast date to predict which deals will slip
- **Slack** — Posts deal momentum alerts to team channels so managers and peers see at-risk deals early

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your meetings live in Harmony (the skill analyzes call transcripts for engagement depth and sentiment) and your CRM is current. Without meeting transcripts, the skill calculates momentum from meeting frequency and CRM activity, which is less precise but still useful.

## What You Get

A momentum report with: overall momentum score (0–100), current status (accelerating/stable/declining), detailed analysis of meeting frequency, stakeholder involvement, commitment clarity, and sentiment, a momentum scoring breakdown showing which areas are strong and which are weak, and an intervention strategy if momentum is declining (specific actions to take this week, success criteria, and forecasting recommendation).
