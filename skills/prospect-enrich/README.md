# Prospect Enrichment Analyzer

> Go into every call with complete intelligence — buying signals, decision-makers, and exactly what matters to this prospect.

## What It Does

Builds a comprehensive prospect brief by combining CRM data, company research, and conversation history into one document you read 10 minutes before a call. Instead of scrambling through email threads or spending an hour on web research, you get a clear map of the prospect, their company, who influences decisions, what problems matter to them, and what's changed since you last talked.

## Who It's For

- **Sales representatives** preparing for discovery or follow-up calls without diving through email and LinkedIn
- **Sales managers** who need reps to walk into calls with full context and competitive intelligence
- **Account executives** working deals in competitive situations where knowing who influences decisions matters
- **Sales development reps** building lead lists and identifying which prospects have the highest buying signals

## Use Cases

- 30 minutes before a discovery call, you have zero context on the prospect — get a full brief in 5 minutes
- Following up with a prospect after a 3-month gap — see what changed in their company or role that creates urgency
- Competitive deal where you need to know who influences decisions and what they care about — understand the buying committee before the call
- Preparing for a proposal review meeting — know their financials, recent news, and competitive context so you can frame the value

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — Pulls contact history, company records, and past activity to establish baseline relationship and interaction patterns
- **Gmail** — Extracts email threads with the prospect to surface specific challenges they've mentioned and tone of engagement
- **Google Calendar** — Shows past meeting history and attendee patterns to understand who influences decisions at this company
- **LinkedIn** — Identifies recent role changes, company news, and team composition to spot new urgency or personnel changes

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your CRM has rich contact history and recent interactions, and when your past conversations are captured in Harmony so the skill can pull specific context about what this prospect cares about.

## What You Get

A one-page prospect brief covering: contact overview, company fundamentals, buying committee map, buying signals and urgency indicators, conversation history, competitive context, red flags, call preparation with specific questions and positioning recommendations, and immediate next steps. Takes 5 minutes to read, saves you an hour of research.
