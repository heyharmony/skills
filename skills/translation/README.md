# Conversation Translation

> Break the language barrier — translate any Harmony conversation content while keeping every speaker, timestamp, and decision intact.

## What It Does

Translates Harmony transcripts, meeting summaries, action items, and conversation-derived content into your target language. Preserves speaker attribution, formatting, timestamps, and tone — so the translated version is just as actionable as the original.

## Who It's For

Global teams and multilingual organisations who need conversation outputs in a language the meeting wasn't conducted in — without losing context or spending time on manual translation.

## Use Cases

- English-language sprint retro finishes → Spanish-speaking product team receives the full summary with decisions and action items in their language, ready to act on.
- Client call conducted in French → account team gets an English translation of key moments and commitments to update the CRM.
- Multilingual all-hands with mixed English and German → unified transcript in a single language for company-wide distribution.

## Setup

Install and go. Works with any Harmony content — transcripts, summaries, notes, or action items. No language packs or additional configuration needed.

## What You Get

A Markdown-formatted translation preserving the original document structure (headings, tables, bullet lists), with speaker names, timestamps, and proper nouns intact. Includes translation notes for any terms left untranslated, cultural adaptations, or ambiguities.
