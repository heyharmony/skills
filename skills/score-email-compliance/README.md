# Score Email Compliance

> Audit your email program against GDPR, CAN-SPAM, CASL, CCPA, and Australian Spam Act — get a compliance score, identify violations ranked by risk, and a remediation timeline to legal safety.

## What It Does

Score Email Compliance systematically audits your email marketing program against five major global regulations. It examines consent mechanisms, unsubscribe implementation, email content requirements, data retention practices, and cross-jurisdiction risks. You get a numerical compliance scorecard (1–100), a breakdown by regulation, violations ranked by financial and legal risk, and a prioritized remediation roadmap with owners and timelines.

No more guessing whether your email program is legally sound. This skill surfaces critical gaps (like missing consent records for EU subscribers) and medium-risk gaps (like missing List-Unsubscribe headers) in one coherent scorecard that marketing leaders can present to Legal and the board.

## Who It's For

- **Compliance officers and legal teams**: Quantify email program risk and track remediation progress.
- **Marketing operations leaders**: Identify what's blocking compliance and get a timeline to fix it.
- **Email marketers**: Understand why unsubscribe, consent, and retention policies matter and how to implement them.
- **RevOps professionals**: Ensure your subscriber data and CRM practices align with regulations.
- **Founders and CFOs**: Understand the financial risk (up to 4% of revenue in GDPR fines) and timeline to remediation.

## Use Cases

- **Quarterly or annual compliance review**: Map your email program against GDPR, CAN-SPAM, CASL, CCPA, and Australian Spam Act.
- **After a privacy policy update**: Verify your email sending practices still align with published commitments.
- **Expanding into new geographies**: Understand regulatory requirements before sending to EU, Canada, California, or Australia.
- **Security audit prep**: Document consent, unsubscribe, and data retention protocols for auditors.
- **After a compliance complaint**: Investigate what regulation was violated and how to fix it.
- **M&A due diligence**: Assess the target company's email compliance posture and cleanup timeline.
- **Before hiring new legal counsel**: Establish a baseline compliance scorecard for your new lawyer to review.

## Connectors

- **HubSpot**: Pulls consent records, subscription preferences, unsubscribe lists, and email send data for audit.
- **Gmail**: Reviews recent email sends to verify compliance elements (sender identity, physical address, unsubscribe link, subject line accuracy).
- **Notion**: References your privacy policy, data processing agreements, and compliance documentation.
- **Slack**: Surfaces legal team discussions about compliance requirements and policy changes.

## Setup

1. Ensure HubSpot is connected and your consent fields are visible (contact your HubSpot admin).
2. Grant Gmail access to review recent email sends from marketing accounts.
3. Share your privacy policy (Notion doc, PDF, or link) for comparison against email practices.
4. Provide your company's sender location (where HQ is domiciled) and subscriber geography (where recipients live).
5. Share your current email retention and deletion policies (if documented).

## What You Get

- **Compliance Scorecard** (2–4 page Markdown or PDF) with overall score, regulation-specific scores, and risk assessment.
- **Detailed audit findings** for consent, unsubscribe, email content, data retention, and cross-jurisdiction practices.
- **Critical violations** (legal liability, fines, ISP blocklisting) with estimated financial risk.
- **Remediation roadmap** with owner, timeline, and priority (Week 1, Week 2, etc.).
- **Appendix**: Evidence from your emails, CRM, and documentation supporting each finding.

## Common Workflows

- **Quarterly compliance review**: Run a full audit every 90 days to catch new violations as your list and geography expand
- **Pre-expansion check**: Before entering a new market (EU, Canada, California, Australia), verify you meet local requirements
- **Post-incident remediation**: After a compliance complaint, run the audit to identify the root cause and prevent recurrence
- **Board reporting**: Generate a compliance scorecard that Legal and the board can review alongside your email program metrics
- **Vendor due diligence**: Score a potential acquisition target's email compliance posture before closing

Assign ownership (Legal, Marketing Ops, RevOps), set deadlines, and re-audit in 90 days to confirm progress.
