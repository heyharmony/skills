# Product Strategy Canvas

> A complete product strategy in 9 sections — from vision to defensibility — with testable hypotheses built in.

## What It Does

Walks through a 9-section Product Strategy Canvas: vision, market segments, cost positioning, value propositions, trade-offs, key metrics, growth model, capabilities, and defensibility. Then stress-tests the strategy by surfacing critical hypotheses and suggesting low-effort validation experiments. Outputs a one-paragraph strategy summary the whole team can recite.

## Who It's For

Product leaders, founders, and strategy teams who need a structured, coherent strategy document — whether for a new product, a pivot, or an annual planning cycle.

## Use Cases

- Building a strategy for a new product → get all 9 canvas sections filled with coherence checks and testable hypotheses
- Preparing for a strategy offsite → structured input that turns messy thinking into a crisp, one-page canvas
- Post-customer-discovery → strategy grounded in real JTBD and willingness-to-pay signals from Harmony conversations

## Setup

Install and go. Provide the product or business to strategize for, plus any market context, competitive intel, or customer data. For strategies grounded in real signals, point to Harmony conversations (customer interviews, sales calls, investor discussions).

## What You Get

A full Product Strategy Canvas: strategy summary, 9 completed sections, coherence assessment, 3-5 critical hypotheses with confidence levels, and validation experiments with effort estimates.
