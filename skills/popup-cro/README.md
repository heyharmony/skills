# Popup CRO

Popups are your final conversion lever. This skill guides you through designing, deploying, and optimizing modals that turn browsers into leads—without the sleaze.

## What This Does

This skill covers the full popup lifecycle:

1. **Trigger strategy**: When and to whom your popup appears (exit-intent, scroll-depth, time-based, click, behavioral)
2. **Messaging formulas**: Copy that leads with benefit and answers "why now?"
3. **Design principles**: Visual hierarchy, mobile-first layouts, accessibility, close button prominence
4. **Compliance**: GDPR, CCPA, Google interstitial guidelines—so you avoid penalties and reputation hits
5. **Frequency management**: Cap repeat impressions so you build trust, not resentment
6. **Measurement**: Track conversion rates, audience segments, and iterate with A/B tests

## When to Reach For This Skill

- You're redesigning your first exit-intent popup
- Email list growth has plateaued and you need a new lead magnet vehicle
- Stakeholders disagree on popup timing or messaging—you need a framework
- Your popups are converting below 10% and need an audit
- You're launching in a regulated market (EU, California) and need compliance clarity
- Mobile users are abandoning more than desktop—you suspect popup friction

## Key Ideas

**Trigger type matters as much as creative.** A brilliant email-capture headline shown at the wrong moment will flop. Exit-intent + risk-reversal copy beats time-based + generic call-to-action every time.

**Respect your audience.** Popups can feel invasive. Frequency caps, clear exit options, and compelling offers turn them into a feature, not a bug.

**Compliance is competitive advantage.** GDPR-first design builds trust; Google's interstitial rules affect mobile ranking. Both are wins, not overhead.

**Measure before you scale.** Test in-market for 1–2 weeks (min. 1K impressions) before rolling out to all traffic. A/B test one variable at a time.

## Connectors

- **HubSpot**: Track popup conversions, email list quality, and suppress converted visitors
- **Slack**: Get alerts on popup performance and flag underperforming variations
- **Gmail**: Monitor email deliverability post-capture (key signal of list quality)
