# Delegate Work Effectively

> Give people full context and ownership, not control. Develop your team while you scale your impact.

## What It Does

This skill helps you delegate work in a way that builds ownership culture, develops people's judgment, and actually reduces bottlenecks instead of creating new ones. It balances context (why, constraints, success metrics) with autonomy, and shifts you from doer to editor.

Includes delegation briefs that communicate the full picture, ownership agreements that clarify authority and escalation, and calibration frameworks to know when to trust and when to increase oversight.

## Who It's For

- Leaders transitioning from individual contributor to manager/editor
- Anyone who feels bottlenecked because people constantly ask for approval
- Managers building ownership culture in their teams
- Leaders learning to delegate high-stakes work without micromanaging
- Technical leads or senior ICs stepping into mentorship and leadership
- Founders and early-stage leaders learning to trust their teams
- Experienced managers wanting to push decisions further down

## Use Cases

- Handing off a project or responsibility with clear ownership and context
- Scaling your impact by giving away work that develops others
- Transitioning from "doer" to "editor" and learning to let go
- Building judgment in high-performers by pushing decisions down
- Clarifying approval chains and escalation triggers to speed up execution
- Preparing someone for a bigger role by expanding their current responsibilities
- Reducing meeting load by delegating facilitation and decision-making
- Developing bench strength in your team through strategic skill-building assignments

## Connectors

- **Linear** – Syncs issues, ownership assignments, and progress tracking
- **Slack** – Enables async delegation notes and status updates
- **Notion** – Stores delegation context, ownership agreements, and calibration notes
- **GitHub** – Tracks code ownership and delegated PR processes

## Setup

1. Connect Linear or your task management system for ownership tracking
2. Link Slack for async delegation updates and status communication
3. Create a Notion database for delegation briefs and accountability docs
4. Before delegating high-stakes work, use the skill to structure the conversation

## What You Get

- Delegation brief that communicates context, not control (why, constraints, success metrics, decision framework)
- Clear ownership and approval chain so people know what they own vs. what needs your input
- Escalation triggers that tell people when to loop you in (specific events, not permission-seeking)
- Calibration framework to know when to trust and when to dial up oversight
- Regular check-in cadence that builds competence without micromanagement (light touch, not hovering)
- Documentation that creates accountability and clarity over time
- Decision-making framework to help them make choices independently without waiting for your approval
- Recovery plan guidance for when delegated work hits problems (how to escalate, when to ask for help)
