# MEDDIC Call Prep Automator

> Walk into every call with account research done, objections prepped, and MEDDIC questions ready — no scrambling, no blanking, just confident discovery.

## What It Does

Automatically pulls account context, stakeholder research, previous objections from Harmony, and industry patterns, then generates a ready-to-go call brief with your MEDDIC questions pre-written and objection responses prepared. Instead of 30 minutes of research before a call, you get a 5-minute skim-and-go brief that surfaces everything you need. When Harmony has similar calls, it shows you how top performers handled the same objections. When it doesn't, it uses industry knowledge and role-based patterns to prime you for what's likely to come up.

## Who It's For

- **Sales reps** who do back-to-back calls and don't have time for research but want to be prepared
- **Newer reps** learning the MEDDIC framework and needing structure for every call
- **Sales managers** who want their team following a consistent discovery process
- **High-velocity teams** that need to scale call prep across many reps and accounts

## Use Cases

- 30 minutes before a discovery call → Quick research brief with prepared questions ready to go
- First call with a new account → Deep research on company, stakeholder role, likely objections
- Back-to-back calls in a day → Pre-call brief for each call pulled automatically from CRM + Harmony
- High-stakes call (large deal, executive, past proposal) → Maximum prep including battle-tested objection responses
- Sales rep training → New hire reviews 5 prep briefs to understand the discovery process before their first call

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Salesforce / HubSpot** — Pulls account history, opportunity stage, previous activities, and company profile to ground research in real CRM data
- **Harmony meetings** — Analyzes previous calls with this account or similar companies to surface objections you've heard before and how top performers handled them
- **Google Calendar** — Confirms attendee details, maps stakeholder relationships, and reviews meeting history to understand previous interactions
- **Gmail** — Surfaces email threads with the stakeholder to understand tone, concerns, and previous commitments

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your CRM is current (recent activity, deal stage updated) and your Harmony calls are indexed. If you're missing either, the skill generates a brief from whatever data is available, but it's stronger with full context.

## What You Get

A 5-minute prep brief with: company snapshot, stakeholder profile with likely concerns, previous interactions summary, top 3 objections you'll probably hear with prepared responses, a full MEDDIC-aligned call agenda with 6–7 prepared questions, and a success metric for the call. Everything is write-once-use-many: read it once on the way to the call, reference it during if you need to, and you've got MEDDIC structure baked into your discovery.
