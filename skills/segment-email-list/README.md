# Segment Email List

> One generic email to 85K subscribers: 18% open rate. Same list segmented by engagement and behavior: 26% open rate. That's the power of smart segmentation.

## What It Does

Designs an email segmentation strategy from the ground up using RFM scoring (Recency, Frequency, Monetary value) to group subscribers into engagement tiers (Champions, Loyal, Active, At-Risk, Dormant). Layers on behavioral signals (product usage, purchase history, content interest) and zero-party data (signup surveys, preferences). Produces a segment map with targeting rules, estimated lift, content strategies per segment, and automation workflows.

## Who It's For

**Marketing leaders** looking to improve email ROI or reverse declining open rates.
**Email managers** planning a segmentation overhaul or quarterly list refresh.
**Growth teams** optimizing new subscriber nurture workflows.
**E-commerce brands** wanting to increase order frequency and AOV through smarter targeting.
**B2B SaaS teams** selling into different account tiers (SMB vs. enterprise).

## Use Cases

- **Open rate stuck at 18%** — Segmentation alone can lift to 24-28%
- **One-size-fits-all emails underperforming** — Target Champions vs. Dormant differently
- **High unsubscribe rate** — Segment out Dormant; re-engage At-Risk with focused messaging
- **Cold list imported; needs nurture path** — Build separate welcome sequence with gradual segmentation
- **Sales says marketing emails don't match deals** — Align segments to buyer personas and account tiers
- **Need to launch re-engagement campaign** — Identify At-Risk cohort and build win-back series
- **Quarterly email strategy refresh** — RFM scores change; update segment rules every 3-6 months

## What You Get

- **Segment map** — 5 engagement tiers (Champions through Dormant) with RFM ranges, sizes, and send frequencies
- **Content strategy table** — For each segment: email themes, subject line tone, frequency, expected performance
- **Automation rules** — How subscribers move between segments (e.g., "3 opens in 30 days" → promote from At-Risk to Active)
- **Implementation roadmap** — Phased launch plan (build, test, monitor)
- **Performance projections** — Expected open rate, click rate, and revenue lift post-segmentation
- **Success metrics dashboard** — Weekly KPIs to track per-segment performance

## Common Workflows

- **Quarterly segment refresh**: Re-run RFM scoring every 90 days as subscriber behavior shifts — subscribers move between tiers
- **Pre-campaign targeting**: Before a major campaign, pull your Champion and Loyal segments for highest-impact sends
- **Win-back prep**: Use the Dormant and At-Risk segments as input for the Design Win-Back Campaign skill
- **New product launch**: Create a behavioral segment of subscribers who've shown interest in the product category
- **Revenue forecasting**: Use segment sizes and historical conversion rates to project campaign revenue by tier

Typical engagement gain: +6–10 percentage points on open rate. DTC ecommerce typical gain: +15–40% revenue per email sent.
