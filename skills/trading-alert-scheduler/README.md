# Trading Alert Scheduler

> Turn investment decisions into automated alerts so you never miss a trigger to act.

## What It Does

Schedules trading alerts and portfolio rebalancing reminders based on target prices, performance thresholds, and decisions made in investment committee meetings. When conversation data exists from board discussions or portfolio reviews, the skill extracts the exact price targets and trigger conditions that were decided. Investors get real-time price alerts, scheduled portfolio reviews, and automated reminders so decisions made in meetings actually translate into action.

## Who It's For

- **Portfolio managers** monitoring multiple positions and needing alerts for decision thresholds
- **Investment committees** scheduling quarterly portfolio reviews and tracking position performance
- **Angel investors** watching specific positions and waiting for funding announcements or exit signals
- **Finance leaders** managing rebalancing schedules and portfolio governance

## Use Cases

- Investment committee decides to exit if price hits $X — convert that decision into an automated alert so you're notified the moment it happens
- Need to add to a position if it drops to Y valuation — set threshold alert so you're not manually checking prices daily
- Quarterly portfolio review needed but no calendar reminder — schedule it automatically every three months
- Series B announcement coming, and you want to decide whether to participate — set up an alert for when the announcement drops

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Slack** — Receives scheduled trading alerts, daily portfolio summaries, and price threshold notifications throughout trading day
- **Google Calendar** — Align alert schedules with market hours and portfolio review meeting dates
- **Notion** — Stores investment theses and target prices so alerts reference documented decision logic

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when you have clear price targets and decision rules documented, and when your investment committee meetings are calendared so alerts can sync with your review schedule.

## What You Get

A structured alert schedule showing all positions, their decision thresholds (add, hold, exit prices), alert frequency (daily, weekly, quarterly), notification channels, and escalation rules for urgent portfolio events. Includes scheduled portfolio review dates and the logic driving each alert so you stay synchronized with what your committee actually decided.
