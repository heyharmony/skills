# Sales Methodology Implementer

> Stop guessing whether your team actually uses the methodology you trained them on — see exactly where they're strong and where they drift, and coach them back on track with evidence from real calls.

## What It Does

Listens to your reps' customer calls and scores them against your chosen sales methodology (MEDDIC, Sandler, SPIN, Consultative, or custom framework), showing exactly where they nailed it and where they missed critical steps. Uses real quotes from calls to prove what happened and what needs to change. Ends with specific, actionable coaching recommendations — not vague feedback, but "next time ask this question" kind of direction that reps can apply immediately.

## Who It's For

- **Sales managers** coaching reps and wondering if they're actually using the methodology you trained them on
- **Sales leaders** trying to understand why some reps hit quota and others don't — often methodology adherence is the differentiator
- **Sales trainers and enablement** who want to reinforce methodology training with evidence from real customer conversations
- **New reps** learning the company's sales process and wanting to see how top performers execute it

## Use Cases

- One-on-ones with struggling reps → "Your Metrics identification was strong, but you missed the Economic Buyer — here's what to ask next time"
- Training new hires → Play a top performer's call, analyze what they did right, have new rep practice those moves
- Post-pipeline review → Discover that low win rates correlate with reps skipping Discovery or Decision Process steps
- Accountability check → Show the team that those MEDDIC training sessions actually matter by analyzing live calls weekly
- Rep self-coaching → Give reps their own call recordings and methodology scorecards so they coach themselves

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Harmony recordings** — Analyzes actual sales calls and customer conversations; pulls exact quotes to show where methodology was applied or missed
- **Salesforce / HubSpot** — Cross-references call coaching with deal outcomes (did reps who score higher on methodology actually win more?)
- **Google Calendar** — Maps upcoming customer calls; enables "prep before calls" coaching (e.g., send rep the methodology checklist before they jump on the call)
- **Slack** — Posts methodology feedback to rep channels so coaching is visible and others learn from it too

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your sales calls live in Harmony — the skill analyzes transcripts or recordings to show exactly what happened. If you only have notes or summaries, it will coach from that but with less precision.

## What You Get

A coaching document with: call overview, methodology adherence scorecard (score for each step plus evidence), detailed feedback on every step (what the rep did well, what to adjust, specific language for the next call), overall coaching summary (top strength, primary gap, next practice opportunity), and concrete "Now What?" action items. Designed for the rep to use immediately — before their next customer call.
