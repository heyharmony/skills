# Pipeline Health Analyzer

> Turn deal data and meeting signals into early warnings — know which deals are at risk before they slip, and exactly what to do about it.

## What It Does

Scores every deal in your pipeline on health and risk, surfacing deals that are aging, losing engagement, or missing stakeholders — before they blow up your forecast. When you have Harmony meeting data, it pulls engagement signals directly from sales calls (buyer sentiment, decision timeline clarity, stakeholder involvement). When you don't, it works from CRM stage and activity data to calculate velocity. Either way, you get a ranked list of at-risk deals with specific interventions your team should take this week.

## Who It's For

- **Sales managers** doing pipeline reviews and forecasting — need to know which deals are actually healthy
- **Sales reps** who want early warning when deals are stalling before their manager notices
- **Sales VPs and RevOps** preparing for board updates or investor meetings, need forecast accuracy confidence
- **Deal coaches and sales enablement** who need data on why deals age and how to fix it

## Use Cases

- Weekly pipeline scrub → Health scores for every deal; flag the 2–3 needing intervention this week
- One-on-one with underperforming rep → Identify stalled deals, coach on engagement cadence and decision path clarity
- Forecast Q-end accuracy check → Which commits are solid, which are at risk, and which will slip to next quarter
- Post-mortem on missed deals → Understand when momentum started declining and what intervention could have helped
- Pipeline clinic before board meeting → Credible forecast with risk quantified and mitigation strategies clear

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Salesforce / HubSpot** — Pull deal stages, created dates, and last activity timestamps to calculate velocity and surface aged deals
- **Harmony meetings** — Analyzes sales calls, discovery meetings, and customer touchpoints to assess engagement depth, stakeholder involvement, and buying signals
- **Google Calendar** — Maps customer meetings to understand cadence and momentum; identifies gaps in scheduled touchpoints
- **Slack** — Posts pipeline health summaries and at-risk alerts to sales team channels for shared visibility

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your sales calls live in Harmony — the skill will pull engagement signals from recordings and transcripts. Without that, it works from CRM data and activity logs to surface velocity and risk.

## What You Get

A pipeline health report with: overall scorecard (healthy/caution/risk/critical deal counts), detailed profiles of every deal scoring below 60 (why it's at risk, what to do immediately, success criteria), engagement cadence tracker (meeting frequency, stakeholder count, decision timeline), stage velocity analysis (which stages are bottlenecks), and forecast confidence quantified with miss risk highlighted. Ends with "Now What?" next steps: coaching calls needed, CRM updates to make, and Slack summaries to share.
