# Content Idea Generator

> Turn every customer conversation into an asset that sells — blog topics, guides, and email sequences rooted in what prospects actually ask.

## What It Does

Generates a prioritized list of content ideas by mining your customer conversations for recurring questions, objections, and pain points. When you have sales calls, discovery meetings, or customer interviews in Harmony, the skill extracts the exact language prospects use, the objections that come up repeatedly, and the information gaps that slow deals down. You get a ranked content calendar with specific angles, formats, and distribution strategies — all backed by evidence of customer demand.

## Who It's For

- **Content marketers** who want to fill content gaps without guessing what to write about
- **Demand generation teams** turning customer objections into nurture assets that advance deals
- **Sales leaders** creating assets their teams actually ask for (not nice-to-haves)
- **Product marketers** building competitive positioning content grounded in real competitor mentions
- **Founders and marketing managers** running lean — prioritizing content that drives revenue, not vanity metrics

## Use Cases

- **Monthly content planning:** 10 discovery calls done this month, need blog topics that will resonate → 8–10 ideas ranked by customer demand and revenue impact
- **Demand generation sprint:** Sales reps keep getting asked "How does this compare to Zapier?" → Competitive comparison guide addresses objection, moves deals forward
- **Sales enablement:** Reps are manually answering "How much time will we save?" on every call → ROI calculator asset answers upfront, shortens discovery
- **Q2 editorial calendar:** Competitors mentioned 20+ times; product just launched 3 new features → Content roadmap addresses competitive gaps and highlights new capabilities with customer evidence

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Harmony conversations** (meetings/calls) — The core data source; extracts customer language, objections, and buying signals directly from transcripts
- **Slack** — Pulls team discussions and customer questions from channels to surface recurring themes and support pain points
- **Gmail** — Surfaces customer email threads and FAQ questions to identify additional content gaps beyond what's covered in calls
- **HubSpot** — References company notes, deal stage patterns, and sales activity to identify which content assets your team is requesting most urgently
- **Notion** — Checks existing content calendar and editorial guidelines to avoid duplicates and maintain consistent voice

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your customer conversations live in Harmony — the skill mines transcripts directly. Without conversation data, provide customer feedback, sales objections, or discovery call notes, and the skill will still identify high-impact topics.

## What You Get

A content calendar ranked by customer demand and revenue impact: top ideas with specific titles, formats, audience, and effort estimates; detailed outlines showing what each asset will cover and how sales teams will use it; themes of unmet customer needs with recommended asset types; competitive intelligence showing which competitors are mentioned and what content could address positioning gaps; and a demand generation map showing which content supports each stage of your sales cycle.
