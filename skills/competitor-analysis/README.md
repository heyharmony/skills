# Competitor Analysis

> Know who you're up against — and exactly where to win.

## What It Does

Identifies 5 direct competitors, maps their strengths, weaknesses, pricing, and positioning, then surfaces concrete differentiation opportunities. When Harmony conversation data is available, it pulls in what prospects and customers actually say about competitors — not just what's on their marketing pages.

## Who It's For

Product managers, founders, and strategy leads who need a structured competitive view — whether for a planning cycle, sales enablement, or investor due diligence.

## Use Cases

- Kicking off competitive research for a new product → get a full landscape map with 5 competitor deep-dives
- Arming the sales team → competitive brief with strengths, weaknesses, and talk tracks grounded in real prospect objections
- Planning a positioning refresh → differentiation opportunities backed by market gaps and conversation signals

## Setup

Install and go. Provide the product or company to analyze and optionally the market segment. For richer insights, point to Harmony conversations where competitors are mentioned (sales calls, demo feedback, churn interviews).

## What You Get

A structured competitive analysis: market overview, 5 competitor profiles (strengths, weaknesses, pricing, threats), differentiation opportunities, and a positioning recommendation.
