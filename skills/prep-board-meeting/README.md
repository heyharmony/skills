# Prep Board Meeting

Prepare for board meetings by synthesizing weeks of internal discussions into a structured agenda, pre-read package, anticipated questions, and follow-up tracker from prior meetings.

## Overview

Board meeting outcomes depend entirely on preparation. This skill pulls together the scattered threads of executive conversations — finance syncs, customer reviews, team updates, strategy discussions — and organizes them into a coherent board package that board members can digest in advance.

Instead of board members walking in unprepared, they receive:
- A clear, time-boxed agenda
- One-page pre-reads for each discussion topic
- Anticipated questions with prepared answers
- Metrics dashboard showing company health
- Context on board member expertise and past positions
- Tracker for action items from the prior meeting

## When to Use

- **2 weeks before scheduled board meeting**: Begin mining recent conversations and documents
- **When assembling board pre-read materials**: Synthesize executive updates into digestible summaries
- **After previous board meeting (for action item tracking)**: Monitor progress on prior commitments
- **Board agenda review with CEO**: Align on priorities and time allocations
- **First board meeting with new board members**: Provide context and background
- **Emergency or special board session**: Quickly compile critical information

## Key Features

**Previous Action Item Tracker**: See which items from the last meeting are complete, in progress, or stalled. Identify blockers and surprises.

**Agenda Structure**: Separate consent items (approve without discussion), discussion items (need board input), and information items (FYI). Time-box each section.

**Pre-Read Summaries**: One-page maximum per topic covering situation, options, recommendation, financial impact, and timeline.

**Anticipated Q&A**: For each discussion item, list 3-5 questions board members likely to ask, with prepared answers grounded in data.

**Metrics Dashboard**: KPIs the board tracks (ARR, NRR, magic number, CAC payback, runway, hiring status). Show trend lines and variance commentary.

**Board Member Notes**: Director expertise, past positions, likely concerns, and new member onboarding context.

**Follow-Up Template**: Action items with owners and deadlines for post-meeting execution tracking.

## Inputs Needed

- Calendar invite for scheduled board meeting
- Last board meeting minutes (to extract prior action items)
- Recent executive sync notes (4-6 weeks back)
- Finance/metrics reports
- Customer feedback or pipeline updates
- Any proposals or decisions pending board approval

## Output

A comprehensive **Board Meeting Prep Package** ready to send to board members 5-7 days before the meeting. Includes agenda, pre-reads, metrics, and follow-up template for post-meeting action tracking.

## Connectors

- **Google Calendar**: Check meeting date and invitees
- **Google Drive**: Store and retrieve board meeting minutes, pre-reads, and prior agendas
- **Notion**: Reference board member profiles or prior meeting notes
- **Slack**: Search executive channel conversations from past 4-6 weeks
- **Gmail**: Find meeting notes and correspondence related to discussion topics
