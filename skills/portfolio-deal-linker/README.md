# Portfolio Deal Linker

> Stop starting from scratch. Surface your best past wins to close deals faster.

## What It Does

Finds the most relevant closed deals from your portfolio when you're working a new opportunity — extracting buyer profile patterns, competitive positioning, objection responses, and deal closure strategies. When conversation data exists from past sales calls, the skill pulls exactly what was said and why the deal closed. Sellers see what worked for similar buyers so they can replicate it, and managers can coach reps on proven strategies instead of generic advice.

## Who It's For

- **Sales representatives** working deals similar to past wins — replicate what worked rather than reinventing
- **Sales managers** coaching reps on deals — pull the playbook from your own wins instead of teaching from scratch
- **Deal strategists and sales leaders** analyzing win patterns across your portfolio
- **Account executives** preparing for discovery calls with prospects who resemble past customers

## Use Cases

- Working a VP Sales at a 500-person SaaS company and want to see how you closed a similar deal last quarter → Find the 3 most similar wins and see what value prop closed it
- Stuck on an integration objection and want to know how your team overcome it before → Surface past deals where that objection came up and what response worked
- Coaching a rep on their first large deal in a new segment → Pull similar closed deals to show what to emphasize and what objections to expect
- Preparing for discovery call with a prospect in a new industry → Find wins in adjacent industries to model questions and value positioning

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Salesforce / HubSpot** — Pulls closed opportunity records, deal amounts, stages, and progression timelines so you can match current prospects to historical wins
- **Gmail** — Surfaces email threads from deal closure to extract proposal language and what messaging actually closed the deal
- **Slack** — Pulls deal celebration messages and team feedback to identify champion profiles and win patterns

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your Salesforce or HubSpot has rich closed opportunity data with contact names, deal amounts, and progression timelines so the skill can accurately match current prospects to past wins.

## What You Get

A structured linkage document showing your 3 most similar past wins with buyer profiles, value props that actually closed them, objections you've seen and proven responses, typical sales cycle length, and a playbook for what worked with this segment. Includes next steps to accelerate the current opportunity using proven patterns from your wins.
