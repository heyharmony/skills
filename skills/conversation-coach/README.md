# Conversation Coach

> Your meetings already hold the feedback you need. This skill gets it out.

## What It Does

Analyzes your Harmony conversations to surface communication patterns you can't see yourself — conflict avoidance, filler words, speaking ratios, listening quality, facilitation habits. Every finding comes with timestamped, cited examples from real meetings, an explanation of why it matters, and a specific "better approach" so you know exactly what to do differently. Track improvement over time by comparing periods.

## Who It's For

Managers, team leads, and professionals who want honest, data-grounded feedback on how they communicate — without waiting for a 360 review or hiring an executive coach.

## Use Cases

- Reviewing a month of meetings → get a full communication profile with strengths, patterns, speaking stats, and 3 prioritized growth areas
- Checking a specific behavior ("when do I avoid conflict?") → timestamped examples with direct-vs-hedged comparisons and concrete practice advice
- Tracking improvement over time → compare Q1 vs. Q2 communication metrics to see what's changing and what still needs work

## Setup

Install and go. Works best with multiple Harmony meeting recordings (the more meetings, the stronger the pattern detection). Specify which person to analyze and optionally which behavior to focus on. For comprehensive profiles, let the skill analyze all dimensions.

## What You Get

A structured coaching report: analysis summary, key patterns with cited examples and "better approach" alternatives, communication strengths, speaking statistics with benchmarks, ranked growth opportunities with practice exercises, and recommended next steps.
