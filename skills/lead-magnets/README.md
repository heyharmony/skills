# Lead Magnets

> Turn your best ideas into the gated content that captures emails, qualifies leads, and feeds your sales pipeline.

## What It Does
This skill guides you through planning and launching gated content (lead magnets) that attract the right prospects and convert them into leads. It covers choosing the right format (checklist, template, quiz, guide, webinar), designing the gating strategy (full gate vs. partial gate vs. content upgrade), building the landing page, setting up delivery and distribution, and measuring what works.

Built on the principle that your best lead magnet topics come from your actual prospect conversations—the questions they ask, the objections they raise, the knowledge gaps they reveal. But this skill works with or without conversation data, giving you a proven playbook to start.

## Use Cases
- You're running a product launch and need to generate pre-launch interest
- Your sales team is hitting a specific objection repeatedly (great magnet topic)
- You have a 30-page blog post that could become a gated guide or template
- You want to segment your email list by buyer stage or product interest
- You're testing new markets and need demand signals before committing budget
- You've had customer interviews and want to validate demand with gated content

## How It Works
The skill walks you through seven steps:

1. **Audit buyer data** – Review customer conversations to identify magnet topics
2. **Match format to stage** – Choose a format (checklist, quiz, template, etc.) that fits your audience's buying stage
3. **Choose gating strategy** – Decide how much friction (full gate, partial gate, content upgrade)
4. **Design landing page** – Create a high-conversion page with minimal form fields
5. **Plan delivery** – Email, PDF download, webinar platform, or portal
6. **Build distribution** – Layer owned, earned, and paid channels
7. **Set measurement** – Define conversion rate, CPL, and lead-to-customer targets

Each step includes decision frameworks, not just templates. You'll output a complete plan with a magnet recommendation, content outline, gating strategy, distribution timeline, and measurement approach.

## Setup
No setup required. Just bring:
- Your single biggest prospect pain point (from sales calls, support tickets, or customer interviews)
- Your target buyer stage (awareness, consideration, or decision)
- Your goal (email list size, CPL target, or lead volume)

If you have access to Harmony conversation data (customer calls, sales recordings, support tickets), use it—it's the fastest way to uncover magnet topics that actually resonate. But the skill works standalone too.

## Real Example
A B2B HR tech company created a 2-minute assessment quiz ("Is Your D&I Hiring Process Mature?") to generate awareness-stage leads for their new Diversity module. They gated it fully (email required), promoted it to warm leads and their existing customer base, and measured 35% landing page conversion and $18 cost-per-lead. They validated demand and generated 40 sales meetings in the first month.
