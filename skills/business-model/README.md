# Business Model Canvas

> Map how your business creates, delivers, and captures value — all 9 blocks, internally consistent.

## What It Does

Generates a complete Business Model Canvas covering all 9 building blocks: Customer Segments, Value Propositions, Channels, Customer Relationships, Revenue Streams, Key Resources, Key Activities, Key Partners, and Cost Structure. Includes a unit economics viability check (LTV:CAC), an alignment assessment across blocks, and a numbered list of assumptions with risks. When conversation data is available, grounds financial and channel decisions in what customers actually say about pricing, buying preferences, and relationship expectations.

## Who It's For

Founders, product leaders, and strategy teams who need to articulate how a business works end-to-end — whether for a new venture, an investor deck, or a strategic review of an existing model.

## Use Cases

- Designing a business model for a new product → get all 9 blocks filled with viability checks and testable assumptions
- Preparing investor materials → structured canvas that shows how value flows from creation to capture
- Reviewing an existing business model after pricing or channel changes → alignment check flags where blocks no longer reinforce each other

## Setup

Install and go. Provide the product or business to model, target customers, and any financial or competitive context. For models grounded in real signals, point to Harmony conversations (pricing discussions, sales calls, partnership conversations).

## What You Get

A complete 9-block Business Model Canvas, unit economics snapshot (CAC, LTV, payback period), block alignment assessment, key assumptions with confidence levels, and recommended validations.
