# Run Brainstorm Session

> Facilitate structured brainstorms that diverge, stress-test, and converge on validated designs — turning rough ideas into actionable concepts.

## What It Does

Runs a proven 8-step brainstorming process to transform vague problems into concrete design options. The skill:

- **Clarifies the problem** — extracts constraints, goals, and success metrics from context
- **Diverges rapidly** — generates 8–12 distinct ideas using creative prompts and reframing
- **Stress-tests thoroughly** — surfaces risks, assumptions, and mitigations for each concept
- **Converges strategically** — ranks ideas by impact × feasibility and selects top 3–4 winners
- **Refines to delivery** — develops winning concepts into 1-page briefs ready for validation or build
- **Prepares for stakeholder review** — structures findings into a clear report and presentation deck

Works from existing context (meeting notes, Slack discussions, Linear issues) or from scratch.

## Who It's For

- **Product Managers** — Exploring feature directions, onboarding flows, or revenue models
- **Founders & Leaders** — Aligned brainstorms with stakeholders before committing resources
- **Design & Engineering Teams** — Collaborative ideation before spec-and-build cycles
- **Process owners** — Redesigning workflows, ops, or team structures

## Use Cases

- **Unblock stuck decisions:** Team has 3 competing ideas; need structured comparison
- **Pre-meeting prep:** Generate 5–6 options to present and discuss with leadership
- **User research follow-up:** Discovered problem in interviews; need 8–10 solutions to prototype
- **Product roadmap planning:** Selecting top 3 initiatives for next quarter
- **Launch readiness:** Designing go-to-market, pricing, or sales strategy

## Connectors

| Connector | Used For |
|-----------|----------|
| **Slack** | Pull team discussions, threads, and ideas to seed brainstorm |
| **Notion** | Reference docs, wikis, decision records for context |
| **Google Calendar** | Identify stakeholder availability and upcoming review meetings |
| **Linear** | Link brainstorm outcomes to project work and engineering sprints |

## Setup

1. **Identify the topic:** What problem or question are you brainstorming?
2. **Gather context:** If available, pull Slack threads, Linear issues, or Notion docs
3. **Block time:** Allocate 90–120 minutes for a live brainstorm or async iteration
4. **Invite stakeholders:** Product, eng leads, customer success (3–6 people optimal)

## What You Get

- **Brainstorm Report** (Notion doc or Google Drive) with:
  - Constraints, goals, and success metrics
  - All divergence ideas (8–12) with 1-line summaries
  - Convergence matrix (impact × feasibility scoring)
  - Top 3–4 refined concept briefs (1 page each)
  - Risk log and mitigations
  - Recommended next steps

- **Optional:** Presentation deck (20–30 min), wireframes or prototypes for top 2 concepts, user testing plan
