# Copywriting

> Clear, compelling page copy that converts — grounded in how your customers actually talk.

## What It Does

Writes structured marketing copy for any page type: homepage, landing page, pricing, feature, about, or product page. Produces organized sections (above the fold, social proof, problem/pain, solution/benefits, how it works, objection handling, final CTA) with headline alternatives, CTA options, and annotations explaining the reasoning. When conversation data is available, mirrors the exact language customers use.

## Who It's For

Founders, product marketers, and growth teams who need conversion-focused page copy — whether launching a new page, rewriting underperforming copy, or adapting messaging for a new segment.

## Use Cases

- Launching a new landing page → get complete structured copy from headline to final CTA with 2-3 headline alternatives
- Rewriting a homepage after repositioning → copy grounded in customer language from Harmony sales calls and interviews
- Creating a pricing page → copy that helps visitors self-select the right plan and overcomes "which is right for me?" anxiety

## Setup

Install and go. Provide the page type, audience, product/offer, and where traffic comes from. For copy that sounds like your customers, point to Harmony conversations (sales calls, interviews, support tickets, onboarding sessions).

## What You Get

Structured page copy organized by section, with headline and CTA alternatives (2-3 each with rationale), annotations explaining key choices, and optional SEO meta content.
