# Champion Identifier

> Map the real decision makers, not just the people you've talked to — so you know who to focus on before the deal gets complex.

## What It Does

Analyzes sales call transcripts to identify who actually decides, who influences, and who blocks. Extracts job titles, authority levels, buying concerns, veto power, and relationships between stakeholders. Surfaces the true decision path so you know who needs to approve what, who's the likely champion, and who's the biggest risk. When Harmony conversation data is available, grounds every identification in direct quotes and behavioral signals from the actual call.

## Who It's For

- **Sales managers** reviewing deals to understand stakeholder structure before coach calls
- **Account executives** preparing for multi-stakeholder negotiations
- **Sales engineers** scoping discovery calls and knowing who to talk to next
- **Deal strategists** working on complex B2B deals with distributed decision making

## Use Cases

- Two calls done at Acme, one with Ops and one with Engineering — who holds final authority? → Stakeholder map showing decision path, veto risks, and who to focus on
- Deal stalled because "someone needs to approve it" — who exactly, and what would convince them? → Detailed profiles of each stakeholder's concerns and winning proofs
- Building a 90-day sales strategy for a 6-month deal — which stakeholders should we involve next? → Gap analysis showing who's missing and when to involve them
- Prepping for contract negotiation — who controls budget, who controls terms, who controls technical approval? → Organizational structure showing coalition builders vs. blockers

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Salesforce / HubSpot** — Syncs identified stakeholders to account and opportunity records so the entire team sees the decision structure without duplicating work
- **Slack** — Posts stakeholder maps to deal channels for real-time alignment across sales, sales engineering, and deal team
- **Linear** — Creates follow-up research tasks for stakeholder gaps and documents recommended next conversations

## Setup

Install and go. Provide a sales call transcript or recording (or point to Harmony conversations), and identify the stakeholders mentioned. The skill extracts authority, concerns, and relationships automatically.

## What You Get

A stakeholder map with profiles of every decision maker and influencer, a hierarchy showing who reports to whom, power dynamics revealing champions and blockers, and a gap analysis with recommendations for which conversations to schedule next.
