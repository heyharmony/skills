# Contact Hunter

> Find the right people to talk to before you cold call or email — so you reach decision makers, not gatekeepers or the wrong person entirely.

## What It Does

Researches target accounts and identifies the key contacts across finance, operations, engineering, and end-user functions. Surfaces names, emails, LinkedIn profiles, job titles, reporting structure, and decision-making roles. Shows you the buying committee structure so you know who to reach out to first, in what order, and why each person matters. Uses LinkedIn, company websites, and org charts to build a complete stakeholder list before outreach.

## Who It's For

- **Sales development reps (SDRs)** building target contact lists for outbound campaigns
- **Account executives** starting research on a new named account
- **Sales leaders** validating buying committee coverage before a big deal
- **Marketing teams** identifying contact lists for multi-touch campaigns

## Use Cases

- Starting outbound to VP Finance at Series B fintech companies → Complete contact list with emails, titles, reporting structure, and reason for each contact
- Strategic account planning at a specific company → Full buying committee map showing decision path and who has veto power
- Following up after a sales call to build the stakeholder coalition → Identify 2–3 other contacts to involve in the next round
- Validating you're talking to the right person → Cross-check title, reporting structure, and decision authority against what the contact told you

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — Import discovered contacts directly into account records; track buying committee coverage and identify missing stakeholders
- **Gmail** — Cross-reference found email addresses against your email history to avoid duplicates and find patterns of who responds in similar companies
- **LinkedIn** — Validate titles and reporting structure; find additional contacts through org relationships and degree connections

## Setup

Install and go. Provide the target company or accounts, the role(s) you're looking for, and any context on the decision-making process (who controls budget, who has technical veto, etc.). The skill returns a ranked contact list with full profiles and outreach strategy.

## What You Get

A complete contact list with names, emails, LinkedIn profiles, titles, reporting structure, buying committee map showing decision path, confidence levels for each contact, and a recommended outreach order with key questions for each person.
