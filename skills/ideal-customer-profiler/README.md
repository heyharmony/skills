# Ideal Customer Profiler

> Stop guessing who to sell to. Turn customer conversations and research data into a structured ICP your whole team can act on.

## What It Does

Synthesizes your customer conversations, survey data, and usage patterns into a complete ideal customer profile — firmographics, behavioral patterns, jobs to be done, pain points, and concrete disqualification criteria. When your customer calls live in Harmony, the profile is grounded in what real customers actually said about their problems, buying process, and desired outcomes.

## Who It's For

- **Founders** defining their first ICP or refining after early traction
- **Sales leaders** who need clear qualification criteria for their team
- **Product managers** aligning roadmap with highest-value customer needs
- **Marketing leads** building targeted campaigns and messaging

## Use Cases

- Done 10+ customer interviews, need to synthesize into a usable ICP → Complete profile with voice-of-customer evidence and team-specific implications
- NPS data shows some segments love you more than others → Value-segmented ICP with data on what makes the best customers different
- Sales team is closing deals that churn → Disqualification criteria and "looks like ICP but isn't" analysis
- Entering a new market and need a hypothesis ICP → Structured v0 profile with a validation plan

## Setup

Install and go. For the richest profiles, have customer conversations in Harmony — the skill pulls verbatim language, buying signals, and pain point descriptions directly from your calls.

## What You Get

A structured ICP document with: one-line profile, demographic and firmographic snapshot, value evidence table, behavioral patterns (discovery, evaluation, triggers, buying signals), jobs to be done (functional, emotional, social), voice-of-customer quotes, ranked pain points, disqualification criteria with false-positive warnings, team-specific implications for sales, marketing, product, and CS, and concrete next steps.
