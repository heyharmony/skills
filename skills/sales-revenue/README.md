# Sales Revenue Tracker

> Know exactly what you've closed, what's in the pipeline, and whether you're on track to hit your target — with confidence levels for every deal.

## What It Does

Pulls your actual closed revenue and analyzes your entire open pipeline to forecast the rest of the month or quarter. Works from your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce) and becomes richer with Harmony conversation data — recent sales calls show which deals are truly ready to close vs. which are stuck. Sales leaders get transparency into "Are we on track?" and "What deals are at risk?" down to the deal level.

## Who It's For

- **Sales leaders** — Weekly/monthly revenue tracking vs. targets and quota planning
- **VPs of Sales** — Accuracy in forecasting and deal health visibility across the team
- **Finance teams** — Revenue projection and commission tracking
- **Account Executives** — Personal revenue dashboard showing quota progress
- **Revenue Operations** — Pipeline health metrics and forecast accuracy measurement

## Use Cases

- Month-end close — track actual revenue and forecast for final days → Segmented view by rep, deal, and stage
- "Are we on track this quarter?" → Pipeline forecast vs. target shows if you'll hit it
- One rep is underperforming → Drill into their deals and forecast; identify at-risk opportunities
- Board update needs revenue numbers → Quick summary of actuals, forecast, and confidence
- Deal slips mid-month — what's the impact? → Re-forecast showing new expected closing date

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — Pull deal stages, amounts, close dates, and opportunity history for accurate forecasting
- **Slack** — Push weekly revenue updates and quota progress to sales channels for team alignment
- **Stripe** — Track actual captured revenue to reconcile pipeline forecast vs. cash received
- **Harmony** — Pull recent sales calls to assess deal momentum and verify forecast confidence

## Setup

Install and go. Best results when your CRM is current (deals tracked by stage, close dates accurate) and you have recent sales call data in Harmony so the forecast reflects real deal momentum, not just historical stage probabilities.

## What You Get

An executive-ready revenue report: actual closed deals by rep and segment, pipeline breakdown by stage, revenue forecast vs. target with confidence, deals at risk (no recent activity, approval uncertain), upsell opportunities, velocity metrics (deal speed, win rate, deal size trends), and conversation insights from recent sales calls showing which deals will actually close.
