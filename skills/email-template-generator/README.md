# Email Template Generator

> Reusable email templates so your whole team sends consistent, tested messaging — not everyone winging it with their own style.

## What It Does

Generates copy-pasteable email templates for sales scenarios — discovery call invites, post-demo follow-ups, objection handling, proposal intros, and closes. Each template includes subject line options, personalization guidance, tactical send-time and tone notes, and expected reply rates. Templates are designed to work repeatedly without heavy customization while still leaving room for thoughtful personalization. Built on what works from past sales conversations.

## Who It's For

- **Sales managers** codifying best practices so the whole team uses winning email patterns
- **Sales development reps (SDRs)** who need consistent, tested templates for outbound
- **Sales enablement teams** building an email template library for the organization
- **Individual reps** who want to improve reply rates by using templates proven to work

## Use Cases

- Building a sales email library for your team → templates for discovery, demo follow-up, objection handling, close, and proposal intro, one per persona
- Scaling a winning email pattern → you've found what works with CFOs; generate variations for VPs and managers
- Onboarding new reps → templates codify best practices so junior reps follow the same winning patterns as top performers
- Improving reply rates → analyze what worked in past deals, turn it into a repeatable template, measure performance

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Gmail** — Store generated templates in Gmail templates or drafts; track which templates get replies
- **HubSpot** — Save templates as workflows; track usage and reply rates by template and persona
- **Slack** — Share templates with sales team so everyone uses consistent messaging
- **Notion** — Create a shared template library where reps can see templates, reply rates, and notes on what works for each persona

## Setup

Install and go. Provide the sales scenario (discovery follow-up, demo invite, objection response, etc.), the buyer persona (title, seniority, company size), and what you want them to do next (meeting, reply, demo). The skill generates a complete template with subject lines, personalization guidance, and tactical notes.

## What You Get

A complete, copy-pasteable email template with subject line options, personalization placeholders with guidance, tone notes for the persona, expected reply rates, when to use it, common variations for related personas, suggested handling for different reply types, and performance tracking recommendations.
