# Edit Marketing Copy Skill

## Overview
**Edit Marketing Copy** is a structured editing skill for Harmony that helps marketing teams strengthen their messaging through systematic review passes. It uses the Seven Sweeps framework—a proven editorial method that evaluates copy across seven dimensions: clarity, brand voice, reader motivation, evidence, specificity, emotional impact, and persuasiveness.

## How It Works
Rather than making random edits, this skill guides you through **seven focused passes**, each targeting a specific quality:

1. **Clarity** — Does the reader understand immediately?
2. **Voice & Tone** — Does copy reflect your brand personality?
3. **So What** — Why should the reader care about each claim?
4. **Prove It** — Is every promise backed by evidence?
5. **Specificity** — Are details concrete, not vague?
6. **Heightened Emotion** — Does copy move readers to action?
7. **Zero Risk** — Are objections addressed and trust built?

## Key Features
- **Grounded in real data** — Uses customer conversations, email patterns, and team voice discussions to make copy authentic
- **Swap-resistant** — Every claim gets validated; every vague statement gets specificity
- **Barrier-focused** — Identifies and removes friction from copy that could prevent conversions
- **Output-ready** — Delivers edited copy with transparent notes on every change

## Connectors
Integrates with your existing tools:
- **Slack** — Extract brand voice discussions and team messaging feedback
- **Gmail** — Analyze customer language for authenticity checks
- **Google Drive** — Reference brand guidelines, style guides, and existing copy
- **Notion** — Access content strategy docs and messaging frameworks

## Getting Started
1. Paste or share the copy you want to edit
2. Provide context (audience, goal, any brand guidelines)
3. The skill runs through the Seven Sweeps, flagging issues and rewriting as needed
4. Review the edited copy and sweep-by-sweep notes
5. Iterate or ship
