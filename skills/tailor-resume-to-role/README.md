# Tailor Resume to Role

> Align your resume to match the job posting — reorder experience, embed keywords, and get a match score with gap analysis.

## What It Does

Transform a generic resume into a targeted application for a specific role. The skill analyzes the job description, extracts must-have requirements and keywords, maps your experience against the JD, identifies gaps and transferable skills, restructures your resume for relevance, and optimizes for ATS screening. Delivers an ATS-ready tailored resume, match score (0-100%), gap analysis, and interview prep guide.

## Who It's For

- **Job seekers** applying for specific roles and needing to optimize resume positioning
- **Career changers** repositioning experience from one industry or function to another
- **Professionals** with large resumes who need role-specific condensing and prioritization
- **Candidates** concerned about ATS filtering or passing initial resume screening
- **C-suite/executive candidates** tailoring for director, VP, or Head roles at specific companies

## Use Cases

- **Direct applications**: Tailoring resume before clicking "apply"
- **Recruiter outreach response**: Customizing resume when approached for a specific position
- **Comparing multiple roles**: Running same resume against 3 competing offers to pick best fit
- **Career transition**: Moving from SaaS to fintech, IC to manager, design to product
- **Gap identification**: Understanding what experience/skills you're missing vs. ideal candidate
- **Interview prep**: Aligning resume narrative to JD priorities for talking points

## Setup

Provide:
1. **Current resume** (full text or document link)
2. **Target job posting** (full JD text—the more detailed, the better)
3. **Your role context** (current title, years of experience, key wins—helps AI understand your positioning)

## What You Get

- **Tailored resume** (ATS-optimized, keyword-rich, restructured for role fit)
- **Match score** (0-100%) with breakdown: must-haves, nice-to-haves, soft skills, keyword coverage
- **Gap analysis**: Hard skill gaps, experience level gaps, industry/domain gaps, recommendations to address each
- **Interview prep brief**: 3 resume stories to prepare, objection-handling talking points
- **Cover letter prompt**: Opening hook, 2-3 key themes to emphasize, how to address gaps narratively

## Example Scenario

**Input**: 6-year Senior PM in fintech seeking "Head of Product" at AI analytics company

**Output**:
- Match score: 71% (Competitive)
- Gaps: 2 years PM experience short, no SQL/Python, limited enterprise sales experience
- Tailored resume: Reordered to lead with enterprise adoption wins, embedded keywords (data, growth, scaling, metrics)
- Interview stories: 85% enterprise adoption scaling, 35% payment failure reduction via data analysis, PM team leadership
- Objection handles: "My 6 years in high-growth SaaS = 10+ years elsewhere; I'm learning SQL now; enterprise SaaS is exciting new domain"

Use when you want data-backed confidence that your resume is positioned optimally before submitting.
