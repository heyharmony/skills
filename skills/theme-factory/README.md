# Theme Factory

> Pick a professional theme. Apply to your artifact. Look polished in minutes.

## What It Does

Theme Factory offers 10 professionally designed color and typography themes you can instantly apply to presentations, documents, reports, or dashboards. Each theme includes a carefully chosen color palette (primary, secondary, accent, neutrals), font pairings, and a visual identity. Pick a theme that matches your industry and tone, follow the application steps, and your artifact is instantly cohesive and polished. No design background needed.

Themes work standalone for one-off projects or as a foundation if you're building brand guidelines from scratch.

- **Startups and small companies:** Quick access to professionally designed color palettes and typography
- **Individual contributors:** Create a report, deck, or proposal that looks intentional and trustworthy
- **Design explorers:** Use themes as inspiration or starting points for defining your own brand
- **Recurring template builders:** Apply a theme to a template once; everyone uses it going forward
- **Event and communication teams:** Quick styling for flyers, announcements, or event materials

## Setup

Install the skill. To apply a theme:
1. Browse the 10 themes (descriptions included in skill)
2. Pick the one that matches your industry, audience, and tone
3. Trigger with *"apply [theme name]"* or *"use [theme name] theme"*
4. Follow the application steps; the skill guides you through colors, typography, and spacing
5. Export your styled artifact

For a one-time theme application, the process takes 10-15 minutes. For recurring templates, invest 20-30 minutes upfront; future uses take 5 minutes.

## What You Get

A professionally styled artifact in one of 10 curated themes. You'll have a color palette and typography pairing that work together, imagery guidance, and a checklist to ensure consistency. The skill also suggests what's next—whether that's saving as a template, sharing with your team, or exporting for presentation or print.

The 10 themes cover most industries and tones: trustworthy (Ocean Depths), warm and creative (Sunset Boulevard), natural and sustainable (Forest Canopy), clean and modern (Modern Minimalist), premium and luxurious (Golden Hour), tech and professional (Arctic Frost), bold and artistic (Desert Rose), cutting-edge and energetic (Tech Innovation), peaceful and inclusive (Botanical Garden), and mysterious and ambitious (Midnight Galaxy).
