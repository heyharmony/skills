# Prospect Research to Outreach Cadence

> Turn research into a personalized sequence that actually gets responses — because "We help companies like you" doesn't work.

## What It Does

Converts prospect research into a credible, multi-touch outreach cadence. Instead of generic templates, the skill creates personalized email, call, and LinkedIn sequences grounded in what you actually know about the prospect — their company signals, job changes, funding, industry news, or prior conversations. When conversation data exists in Harmony, prior calls and buying signals inform the cadence so you're not starting cold. When it doesn't, the skill helps you identify what to look for on your first interaction.

## Who It's For

- **Sales development reps and SDRs** running outreach and want sequences that feel personalized, not templated
- **Sales leaders** scaling outreach and wanting higher response rates from better-researched sequences
- **Account executives** re-engaging prospects who went cold after an initial conversation
- **Founders and sales managers** running lean and wanting to prioritize highest-probability outreach
- **Partnerships and BD teams** reaching out to strategic prospects with a clear, research-backed reason

## Use Cases

- **New prospect:** Research shows they just hired a VP and their company is growing; create a 6-touch cadence that references the hiring + growth signal → Higher credibility, higher response rate
- **Re-engagement:** Prospect attended a webinar, viewed pricing page, didn't reply to initial email; create a follow-up cadence that acknowledges engagement + buying signal → Converts stalled interest into meetings
- **Warm intro prep:** You have a mutual connection who will introduce you; create a concise first message that makes the ask clear and the value obvious → Maximizes warm intro effectiveness
- **Prior call follow-up:** You had a discovery call 6 months ago, went cold, now it's the right time to reach back out; cadence references prior conversation + new company signal → Avoids "cold call" feeling even though time has passed

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — Pull prospect company data, contact history, and prior interactions to avoid duplicate outreach and personalize based on CRM relationship history
- **Gmail** — Track opens, clicks, and replies to monitor cadence effectiveness; update engagement status in real time
- **LinkedIn** — Pull company growth signals, job changes, mutual connections, and recent activity to find the right person to reach out to and the perfect timing

## Setup

Install and go. Provide basic prospect info (name, company, title, ICP fit) and research (company signals, prior conversations if any). The skill creates a sequence. Send it, track responses, and adjust based on what you learn.

## What You Get

A multi-touch outreach sequence: 5–7 specific touches with channel (email, call, LinkedIn), exact timing (day and time), a research-backed reason why you're reaching out, and the exact message template with personalization. Includes response scenarios (if they reply, call, don't respond, say "not interested") and what to do next. Includes tracking metrics and success definition so you know if the cadence is working.
