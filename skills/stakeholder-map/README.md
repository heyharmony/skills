# Stakeholder Map Builder

> Know who matters, how much attention they need, and what to tell them — before a missed stakeholder derails your project.

## What It Does

Maps every stakeholder on a Power × Interest grid and builds a concrete communication plan — frequency, channel, message framing, and conflict resolution strategies for each person. When project meetings exist in Harmony, it identifies stakeholders from conversation patterns: who shows up, who gets mentioned, who raises concerns, and who has authority to block or accelerate decisions.

## Who It's For

- **Product managers** kicking off cross-functional initiatives
- **Project leads** managing complex stakeholder landscapes
- **New leaders** inheriting a project and mapping the political terrain
- **Anyone** whose project stalled because a stakeholder felt surprised or ignored

## Use Cases

- New project kickoff with 15+ people involved → Complete stakeholder grid with tiered communication plans and weekly calendar
- Cross-functional initiative keeps losing alignment → Conflict analysis with specific resolution strategies per tension
- Inherited a project mid-stream → Stakeholder map built from meeting recordings and org context
- Preparing for a launch that touches sales, CS, marketing, and engineering → Role-by-role engagement plan with quadrant movement watch

## Setup

Install and go. For the most accurate maps, have project meetings in Harmony — the skill identifies stakeholders from who attends, who gets mentioned, and who raises direction-setting concerns in conversations.

## What You Get

A stakeholder map with: Power × Interest grid with rationale for each placement, detailed communication plan (frequency, channel, message framing, mutual needs), conflict and alignment risk analysis, quadrant movement watch for stakeholders whose priority may shift, weekly communication calendar, and concrete next steps.
