# Morning Brief

> Start every day knowing your top 3 priorities, which deals are closing, and exactly what to focus on first.

## What It Does

Delivers a daily briefing that surfaces yesterday's sales activity, today's scheduled calls, hot deals closing this week, and priority follow-ups. Pulls from Harmony conversation data (yesterday's sales calls, customer interactions, action items committed), CRM updates (stage changes, new activities), calendar (today's meetings), and email (unread from key accounts). Reps get a 5-minute daily agenda; leaders get a pulse on team momentum; distributed teams get a Slack summary.

## Who It's For

- **Account Executives** — Know your daily priorities and prep for scheduled calls without scrambling
- **Sales Development Managers** — Track team activity and surface blockers or off-track reps
- **Sales Leaders** — Daily pulse on deal momentum, pipeline changes, and team productivity
- **Revenue Ops** — Monitor velocity metrics and identify process blockers in real time
- **Distributed Sales Teams** — Async briefing keeps everyone aligned without daily standup

## Use Cases

- First thing Monday morning → Know what changed over the weekend and what's critical today
- Hot deal closing this week → Track daily progress, identify blockers, keep team focused
- High-activity week (lots of demos, proposals) → Morning brief maps daily priorities so everyone knows their sequence
- New sales rep onboarding → Daily brief gives context for their calls and shows what matters
- Sales leadership checks deal health → Broadcast briefing to Slack shows momentum, at-risk deals, and team activity

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — Surface deals that moved stages, approaching close dates, and recent account activity
- **Google Calendar** — Show today's scheduled meetings and prep time before calls
- **Gmail** — Surface unread messages from key prospects/customers that need replies
- **Harmony** — Pull yesterday's sales calls, customer feedback, and action items from transcripts
- **Slack** — Deliver briefings to team channel each morning for async visibility and alignment

## Setup

Install and go. For best results, connect your CRM (HubSpot or Salesforce) and Google Calendar so the briefing pulls your real meeting schedule and deal updates. If you have Harmony, it pulls call context and action items from yesterday's conversations.

## What You Get

A daily briefing (takes ~5 minutes to read) that includes: top priority for the day, deals closing this week, today's scheduled calls with prep notes, summary of yesterday's important conversations, priority tasks and follow-ups due today, emails that need replies, week-ahead preview with targets, metrics on track/behind, and red flags (stalled deals, at-risk opportunities) plus opportunities to win.
