# Prospect Refresh Tracker

> Stop cold-outreaching old prospects. Identify which ones have changed in ways that matter.

## What It Does

Scans your lost deals and stale prospect list to find who got promoted, moved companies, or had their company raise funding or hire new execs. Instead of random cold follow-ups, you re-engage prospects whose situations have materially changed — with context about what changed and why it now makes sense to talk. Sales teams use this to reactivate sleeping deals efficiently, turning old relationships into new opportunities.

## Who It's For

- **Sales representatives** managing large territories who want to systematically reactivate old prospects
- **Account executives** working lost deals who want to know when to reach back out with fresh context
- **Sales development reps** looking to build pipeline from existing contacts rather than pure cold outreach
- **Sales managers** running quarterly territory reviews to identify quick-win reactivation opportunities

## Use Cases

- Lost deal in 2025 said "not ready yet" — prospect just got promoted, now they have budget authority → Re-engage with that context
- Prospect changed companies six months ago and you haven't tracked them — see where they went and if their new company is a better fit → Reconnect with relevant angle
- Territory review: systematically check which of your 50 stale prospects have undergone material changes → Prioritize high-opportunity re-engagements
- Competitive threat: your competitor just won a deal at a company where you know people → Check if any of your contacts moved there and could become internal champions → Re-engage with insider perspective

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **LinkedIn** — Monitors prospect role changes and company moves so you catch them in real-time instead of manual checking
- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — Tracks prospect history and last interaction date so you understand how long it's been and context from the past
- **Gmail** — Reviews email thread history to see what the previous objection was, so you know whether to re-engage

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your CRM has complete contact history and your lost deals are marked as "not converted" so the skill can filter them efficiently. LinkedIn integration provides real-time alerts for role changes.

## What You Get

A ranked list of old prospects segmented by re-engagement opportunity: high-opportunity (re-engage this week), medium (re-engage this month), and low (monitor but don't push). Each prospect includes what changed, why it matters, recommended re-engagement angle, draft message opener, and timing. Turns manual territory reviews into a data-driven reactivation strategy.
