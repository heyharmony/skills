# Customer Metrics Analyzer

> Know which customers are thriving, which are struggling, and which are ready to expand — before it becomes a churn problem.

## What It Does

Extracts customer health and growth signals from your success calls, check-ins, and support conversations. The skill analyzes what customers actually said about their usage, progress toward goals, satisfaction, and future plans to identify who's ready to expand and who's at risk of leaving. When conversation data is available in Harmony, every health assessment is grounded in what was actually spoken. When it's not, the skill helps you identify what data to collect on your next customer call.

## Who It's For

- **Customer success managers** running accounts and identifying expansion opportunities
- **Sales leaders** planning expansion strategy and allocating resources to high-potential accounts
- **Finance and operations** forecasting expansion revenue and managing churn risk
- **Product teams** understanding feature adoption and customer outcome success from conversations
- **Revenue leaders** getting visibility into customer health and expansion pipeline before quarterly planning

## Use Cases

- **Monthly customer reviews:** 8 enterprise accounts, monthly check-in calls done, need health assessment → Report shows 5 healthy (expand now), 2 stable (support), 1 at-risk (intervention plan)
- **Churn risk mitigation:** Customer said on last call "evaluating alternatives"; want to quantify risk and create recovery plan → Health analysis identifies specific blockers, intervention timeline, and likelihood of save
- **Expansion planning:** 3 customers showing strong adoption signals; want to know who's ready for upsell → Report prioritizes by readiness and revenue impact ($1.2M expansion pipeline identified)
- **Cohort analysis:** Q4 onboarded customers appear healthier than Q3 cohort; want to understand why → Trend analysis shows adoption patterns, usage frequency differences, feature adoption gaps by cohort

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Harmony conversations** (monthly check-ins, QBRs, support calls) — Core data source; extracts usage mentions, goal progress, satisfaction signals, and expansion language directly from transcripts
- **HubSpot** — Correlates conversation sentiment with account health scores and interaction history
- **Stripe** — Cross-references customer-reported outcomes with actual usage and revenue trends to validate health claims
- **Slack** — Pulls customer success team notes and customer channel activity to track engagement and sentiment shifts
- **Notion** — References account plans and success plans to measure progress toward stated customer goals

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when your customer check-ins and success calls are recorded and transcribed in Harmony — the skill mines actual conversation language for usage signals, sentiment, and expansion mentions. Without Harmony data, pull your most recent customer communication notes and check-in summaries.

## What You Get

A customer health scorecard showing distribution of health across your base (healthy, stable, at-risk, critical), individual customer assessments with specific signals driving each health score, expansion pipeline with revenue potential and readiness timing, churn risk summary for accounts needing immediate attention, adoption trends by customer cohort, and feature adoption gaps that might indicate need for better onboarding or product improvements.
