# Meeting Prep Brief

> Never walk into a meeting cold again. Get a 2-minute brief with attendee history, open items, and a suggested agenda — pulled from your real conversations.

## What It Does

Builds a pre-meeting brief by pulling together everything relevant from past conversations with the same people: what was discussed, what was decided, what's still open, and what each person cares about. Connects with Google Calendar to identify upcoming meetings and personalize the brief with attendee names, roles, and meeting context.

## Who It's For

- **Anyone** with back-to-back meetings who needs context fast
- **Sales reps** preparing for customer follow-ups and prospect calls
- **Managers** preparing for 1:1s and wanting to pick up where they left off
- **People stepping in** for a colleague's meeting and needing the full backstory

## Use Cases

- "Prep me for my 3pm call with Acme" → Brief with attendee history, trial status, open action items, and talking points for the SSO blocker
- "What should I know before my weekly sync?" → Open items from last week, status updates, suggested agenda
- "I'm covering for Maria in her client call" → Full relationship history, past commitments, communication style notes
- Meeting starts in 5 minutes → Speed prep: 5 bullet points with the essentials

## Setup

Install and go. For calendar-aware prep, connect Google Calendar. The skill pulls from your meetings in Harmony and calendar events to build the most complete brief.

## What You Get

A structured prep brief with: quick context summary, attendee profiles with last interaction and communication style, changes since last meeting, open items tracker, suggested agenda (must-cover, should-discuss, watch-out), talking points with objection handling, and relevant quotes from past conversations.
