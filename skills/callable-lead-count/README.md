# Callable Lead Counter

> Know exactly how many prospects are ready to call right now — no guessing, no stale data.

## What It Does

Identifies and counts leads ready for outbound sales engagement, segmented by company size, industry, and pipeline stage. Works from your CRM data (HubSpot, Salesforce) and becomes richer when Harmony conversation data exists — pulls information about recent calls, email engagement, and buying signals so you count not just leads, but *warm* leads. Sales leaders get a clear answer to "How many leads can our team work this week?"

## Who It's For

- **VP Sales** — Track callable pipeline health and staffing requirements for outbound capacity
- **Sales Development Managers** — Know how many qualified leads each team member should work
- **Revenue Operations** — Monitor lead supply vs. outbound demand to manage headcount and hiring
- **Sales Directors** — Weekly pulse on which segments are hottest and where to focus effort
- **Account Executives** — See your personal callable pool and prioritize high-intent accounts

## Use Cases

- Leadership wants a weekly pipeline pulse → Segmented count showing total, high-priority, and by segment
- Scaling outbound team — evaluate pipeline depth → Count shows you have 47 callable leads but capacity for 60; hire accordingly
- Campaign ended with 200 inbound leads → How many are actually callable vs. future nurture? → Immediate count removes the guessing
- One quarter weak on outbound → Where did leads go? → Breakdown by stage shows bottleneck (e.g., stuck in demo phase)
- Planning sales capacity → How many calls can each rep handle? → Compares your callable pool to team capacity

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot** — Pull deal/contact records, pipeline stages, and custom lead scoring to automatically identify callable prospects
- **Salesforce** — Import opportunity stages, account records, and lead status to segment by readiness
- **Gmail** — Track recent email engagement and response patterns to verify leads are actually responsive
- **Slack** — Push weekly callable counts and hot segments to sales channels for team alignment and visibility

## Setup

Install and go. Best results when your CRM is current and you have recent conversation data in Harmony — the skill will pull call context and email patterns to verify leads are actually warm, not just theoretically qualified.

## What You Get

A weekly callable lead report: total count, segmentation by company size and industry, high-priority leads with names and next actions, velocity indicators (new leads per week, response rates, conversion rates), conversation quality assessment, and capacity planning data. Includes red flags (stalled leads, inactive segments) and recommendations for sourcing or re-engagement.
