# Email Drafting

> Turn any Harmony conversation into a polished, ready-to-send email — no blank-page syndrome required.

## What It Does

Takes the context from a recorded conversation and drafts a professional email tailored to your audience and purpose. Every claim, commitment, and next step traces back to what was actually said — so you never misquote or forget a detail.

## Who It's For

AEs sending post-call follow-ups. PMs recapping decisions for absent stakeholders. Anyone who regularly translates conversations into written communication and hates the context-switching.

## Use Cases

- Sales discovery call ends → polished follow-up email with confirmed next steps and buyer commitments, ready in seconds.
- Internal planning meeting wraps up → concise recap to the broader team with decisions and owners sourced from the transcript.
- Client raises a question during a call → detailed response email grounded in what was discussed, with the right tone for the relationship.

## Setup

Install and go. Works with any conversation recorded in Harmony. For best results, ensure the meeting has a transcript or summary available.

## What You Get

A Markdown-formatted email draft with subject line and body, tailored to the specified recipient and tone. Structured for easy copy-paste or delivery via Harmony's integrations (Slack, HubSpot, etc.).
