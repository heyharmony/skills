# Meeting Recap & Follow-Up

> Get off a call and immediately have a structured summary, clear action items, and a follow-up email ready to send — all in one step.

## What It Does

Turns any meeting into a structured recap — decisions, action items with owners and deadlines, key discussion points, open questions, and notable quotes — then drafts a follow-up email you can send to attendees right away. Works for customer calls, team syncs, 1:1s, interviews, workshops, or any conversation recorded in Harmony.

## Who It's For

- **Anyone** who just got off a call and needs to close the loop fast
- **Sales reps** sending follow-ups to prospects after demos or discovery calls
- **Managers** documenting decisions and holding teams accountable on action items
- **People who missed a meeting** and need to catch up quickly

## Use Cases

- Just finished a customer demo → Recap with buyer signals, commitments, and a professional follow-up email ready to send
- Weekly team sync just ended → Decisions, action items with owners, and blockers documented in 30 seconds
- Missed a meeting → Ask "what happened in the Acme call?" and get the full picture
- 1:1 with a report → Private recap with feedback given, action items, and career development notes

## Setup

Install and go. Works with any meeting recorded in Harmony. The skill pulls the transcript, extracts structure, and drafts the email — you just review and send.

## What You Get

A structured meeting recap with decisions, action items (owner + deadline), key discussion points, open questions, notable quotes, and a parking lot — plus a ready-to-send follow-up email tailored to the audience (customer, internal team, leadership, or 1:1).
