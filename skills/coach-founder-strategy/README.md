# Coach Founder Strategy

A Harmony marketplace skill that acts as a strategic mirror for startup founders, synthesizing decision patterns across meetings to surface blind spots and guide stage-appropriate coaching.

## Overview

Founders make dozens of critical decisions per week across investor calls, team meetings, customer conversations, and board discussions. This skill connects the dots by identifying recurring themes, contradictions between stated and actual priorities, and unresolved decisions that keep looping across conversations.

The result: a Strategic Mirror Report that surfaces what founders keep saying but haven't acted on, where time allocation diverges from priorities, and which decisions are overdue.

## What It Does

- **Synthesizes conversation patterns** — Reviews recent calls, meetings, and discussions to identify themes appearing 3+ times
- **Maps priorities to reality** — Compares what founders say matters most against actual time spent in different areas
- **Surfaces blind spots** — Highlights contradictions, unresolved decisions, and decision velocity bottlenecks
- **Stage-aware coaching** — Adjusts frameworks based on company stage (pre-seed → Series C+)
- **Generates reflection questions** — Prompts founders to think through their own answers rather than prescribing solutions

## When to Use

- Monthly strategic reflection to connect patterns across conversations
- Post-fundraise priority resets
- Before or after pivots
- Quarterly planning cycles
- Co-founder alignment checks
- After tough board meetings

## How It Works

1. Review your recent conversations (10–15 calls across investor, team, customer, and board contexts)
2. Skill identifies recurring themes, contradictions, and unresolved decisions
3. Analyzes priority-action gaps and decision velocity
4. Delivers Strategic Mirror Report with coaching questions and 30-day focus areas

## Role & Action

- **Role:** Leadership & Finance
- **Action:** Analyze

## For Harmony Users

This skill is designed for founders who want strategic clarity without hiring an executive coach. It's most valuable during periods of rapid change—post-fundraise, pre-pivot, or when priorities feel scattered. Best used monthly as part of a founder's strategic cadence.

Pair with co-founder syncs or board meetings for maximum impact.
