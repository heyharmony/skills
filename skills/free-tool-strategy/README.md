# Free Tool Strategy

> Build owned tools your audience wants, capture high-intent leads, and turn customer friction into competitive moat.

## What It Does

This skill guides you through designing, validating, and launching free interactive tools (calculators, generators, analyzers, graders, testers) that solve real customer problems while capturing qualified leads. It's "engineering as marketing" — you identify a pain point from sales calls, support tickets, or Slack discussions, validate that people actually want a tool to solve it, and ship an MVP in 4 weeks.

The skill walks you through:
- Identifying tool opportunities from customer pain points
- Running a quick validation scorecard (search demand, build effort, data availability)
- Choosing the right tool type and lead-capture model
- Scoping an MVP spec and build approach (custom, no-code, white-label)
- Planning launch, promotion, and measurement

## Who It's For

- **Marketing leaders** building lead-gen motion beyond webinars and content
- **Product marketers** who want to own a keyword and educate users through interaction
- **Sales enablement teams** tired of building one-off spreadsheets for every demo
- **Founders and growth teams** seeking low-CAC lead sources that also build brand authority
- **Marketing operators** managing tool build timelines, measurement, and post-launch iteration
- **Revenue teams** aligned on tool concept and lead quality expectations before engineering starts

## Use Cases

- **Sales efficiency:** Reps spend 30 minutes building a ROI model in every discovery call. A calculator replaces that spreadsheet and captures intent early.
- **Self-service scaling:** Support team answers "how do I know if [problem]?" weekly. A grader or diagnostic tool eliminates tickets and uncovers product gaps.
- **Keyword ownership:** You want to rank for "ROI calculator" or "email deliverability tester" but no one in your category has built a good tool. You own it.
- **Audience education:** Prospects don't yet know they have a problem. A free quiz or analyzer educates them and surfaces their gaps (moving them from unaware to aware).
- **Partner amplification:** A useful tool becomes embeddable in partner sites, newsletters, and industry roundups. Your tool does the marketing work.
- **Content recycling:** Your blog post on "5 Payment Processing Metrics" underperforms. Wrap the same content in a calculator and see 10x engagement and leads.

## Setup

1. Go to the Instructions section in SKILL.md and work through the 7-step discovery and validation process
2. Complete the Output Format template, starting with your top 1–2 pain-point candidates
3. Run the Validation Scorecard on each; pick the one that scores ≥3.5 average
4. Share the tool concept with your sales and product teams; validate with 5 customers informally
5. Assign an owner (marketing or product), lock MVP scope, and open build tickets
6. Set up GA4 tracking and a measurement dashboard before launch
7. Launch via owned channels (newsletter, sales enablement, community) in week 1–2
