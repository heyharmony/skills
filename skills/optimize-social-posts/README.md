# Optimize Social Posts

> Turn draft posts into algorithmic wins—score hooks, analyze engagement triggers, and get 3 platform-specific variants ready to publish.

## What It Does

Analyze any social media draft (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Threads) and generate actionable optimization recommendations. The skill scores your original post (1-100), identifies structural and engagement gaps, and delivers three distinct rewrite variants optimized for different audiences and tones. Each variant includes platform-specific algorithm insights, hashtag strategy, and optimal posting timing.

## Who It's For

- **Marketing leaders & content strategists** refining multi-channel campaigns
- **Founders & executives** building personal brands and thought leadership
- **Content creators** scaling engagement across platforms
- **Product marketers** launching features or announcements
- **Sales professionals** amplifying outreach and personal brand

## Use Cases

- **Pre-publish validation**: Draft a post, run it through this skill, pick the strongest variant
- **Content iteration**: Compare multiple angles (data-driven vs. emotional vs. provocative) to find what resonates
- **Underperformance diagnosis**: Posts not getting traction? Get AI-driven rewrites that activate engagement
- **Cross-platform adaptation**: Optimize announcements for LinkedIn professional voice, Twitter conversational voice, Threads narrative flow
- **Campaign launches**: Product releases, funding announcements, thought leadership threads

## Setup

No special setup required. Provide:
1. **Draft post** (text)
2. **Target platform** (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, or Threads)
3. **Audience segment** (e.g., founders, marketing leaders, engineers)
4. **Campaign context** (optional—feature launch, thought leadership, engagement play, etc.)

## What You Get

- **Original post score** (1-100) with hook breakdown
- **Structure & engagement analysis** (readability, CTA clarity, triggers present/missing)
- **3 rewrite variants** at different tones with individual scores:
  - Data-driven (appeals to logic, quantified benefits)
  - Narrative/emotional (appeals to story and humanity)
  - Provocative/opinionated (sparks discussion and controversy)
- **Publishing strategy**: optimal posting time, hashtags, engagement playbook, companion content ideas
- **Platform-specific insights**: algorithm mechanics, timing data, formatting best practices

## Example Output

**Input**: Marketing VP at design tool wants to announce AI feature on LinkedIn

**Output**:
- Original post score: 42/100 (generic corporate announcement)
- Variant 1 (Data-Driven): 76/100 — leads with 68% reduction in layout review cycles, quantified ROI
- Variant 2 (Narrative): 81/100 — opens with personal anecdote about designer spending 45 minutes on spacing
- Variant 3 (Provocative): 74/100 — challenges "design teams waste 15% on layout busywork"
- Optimal posting time: Tuesday–Thursday, 10:30am ET
- Hashtags: #DesignOps #DesignSystems #AI #ProductLed

## Time Investment

- Small posts (tweet-length): 2-3 minutes
- Longer posts (LinkedIn article or thread): 5-8 minutes
- Heavy iteration (5+ variants): 15-20 minutes

Pick the skill when you want immediate, data-backed optimization guidance before hitting publish.
