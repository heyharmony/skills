# Inbound Lead Qualifier

> Know immediately if an inbound lead is worth sales time or should go to nurture — so your team responds to hot prospects in minutes, not days.

## What It Does

Scores inbound leads in real-time against your ICP and buying signals — company size, role, industry, intent, timeline, and deal potential. Provides a clear recommendation: prioritize for immediate sales follow-up, engage within 24 hours, nurture with automated sequences, or pass because it's not a fit. Uses a simple scoring model (ICP fit + buying intent) that works for any sales motion. Become richer with historical context (if conversation data exists, learns what signals actually predicted closed deals).

## Who It's For

- **Sales managers** who need to know which leads get called in the next hour vs. which can wait
- **Sales development teams (SDRs)** qualifying high volumes of inbound and routing to sales
- **Demand generation teams** learning what percentage of inbound is sales-qualified so they can optimize campaigns
- **Sales operations** ensuring consistent qualification criteria across the team

## Use Cases

- Demo form comes in at 3pm on Friday — should someone call today or wait until Monday? → Immediate qualification: PRIORITIZE (call in next hour) or NURTURE (send to sequence)
- Webinar generates 50 leads — which 10 should sales call first? → Bulk qualify, rank by score, distribute top tier to AEs
- Lead from partner referral — is it a good fit or just a favor? → Validate ICP fit + assess if worth immediate follow-up
- Slow inbound period — is it our criteria being too strict, or is inbound quality actually dropping? → Track average scores month-over-month to see if we're becoming more selective or quality is declining

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **HubSpot / Salesforce** — Sync qualification scores to lead records; auto-route high-scoring leads to sales queue; auto-add lower-scoring leads to nurture workflows
- **Slack** — Get real-time alerts when a high-priority lead comes in (score 8+) so sales can respond within minutes
- **Gmail** — Track which leads you respond to quickly vs. slowly; measure how urgency correlates with conversion

## Setup

Install and go. Provide lead information (company, role, source, what they said they want to do) and your ICP definition (typical company size, industry, use case, decision-maker title). The skill scores the lead and recommends an action.

## What You Get

A scored lead profile showing ICP fit, buying intent, overall qualification score (0–10), and a clear recommendation (Prioritize / Engage / Nurture / Pass) with reasoning, next steps, discovery questions, and deal potential estimate.
