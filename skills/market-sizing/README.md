# Market Sizing — TAM, SAM & SOM

> Estimate your addressable market with top-down and bottom-up approaches — grounded in real data, not guesswork.

## What It Does

Produces a structured market sizing analysis with TAM, SAM, and SOM estimates using both top-down (industry reports, analyst data) and bottom-up (unit economics) approaches. Surfaces key assumptions, flags confidence levels, and gives you a summary table ready for pitch decks or strategy docs.

## Who It's For

Founders, product leaders, and strategy teams who need defensible market sizing — whether for investor pitches, go/no-go decisions, or planning market entry.

## Use Cases

- Preparing a pitch deck → get a complete TAM/SAM/SOM breakdown with sources and reasoning
- Evaluating a new segment after customer discovery calls → market estimates informed by real conversation signals
- Cross-validating internal assumptions → two independent estimation methods highlight where your numbers hold up and where they don't

## Setup

Install and go. For best results, provide the product or market you want to size, any geographic or segment constraints, and point to relevant Harmony conversations (customer interviews, sales calls) for grounded signals.

## What You Get

A structured market sizing report: market definition, TAM/SAM/SOM with dual estimation methods, a summary table, growth projections, and a numbered list of assumptions with confidence ratings.
