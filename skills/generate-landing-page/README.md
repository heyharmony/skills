# Generate Landing Page Skill

Turn customer language into conversion-optimized landing pages.

## Overview

This Harmony marketplace skill generates landing page copy and structure grounded in how customers actually describe their problems and solutions. Instead of starting with marketing jargon, this skill mines sales call transcripts, customer interviews, and product feedback to extract the exact language prospects use — then structures that language into a proven landing page framework.

The result: landing pages with higher clarity, resonance, and conversion rates.

## What It Does

- **Mines customer language** from Harmony transcripts and sales calls
- **Generates hero sections** with outcome-driven headlines that speak to prospect pain
- **Builds social proof blocks** with real customer quotes and metrics
- **Designs feature-benefit grids** tied to actual customer outcomes
- **Creates objection handler sections** addressing real sales call concerns
- **Outputs a markdown wireframe** ready for design and handoff

## When to Use It

- Launching a new product or feature
- Refreshing your homepage or main sales page
- Creating campaign-specific landing pages for demand generation
- Building event or webinar registration pages
- Designing competitive comparison pages
- Rewriting pricing pages to sell value, not just price

## How It Works

1. Provide sales call transcripts, customer interview notes, or Harmony call summaries
2. The skill extracts customer language (problems, outcomes, objections, aha moments)
3. It structures that language into a landing page framework: hero → social proof → features → objections → CTA
4. Output is a markdown wireframe with copy blocks ready for design handoff

## Key Features

- **Customer-centric copy**: Headlines, subheadlines, and CTAs use customer language, not product speak
- **Proven framework**: AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action) structure maximizes conversions
- **Objection handling**: FAQ and concern blocks sourced from actual sales calls
- **Flexible format**: Works for homepages, feature launches, lead capture, pricing pages, and more
- **Design-ready output**: Markdown wireframe with copy, structure, and meta tags

## Example

A SaaS platform for API integrations mines calls where prospects say:
- "I'm tired of building integrations from scratch"
- "We just need it to work without babysitting"
- "Our team spends 40% of sprint time on integration bugs"

The skill generates a landing page with the hero headline: **"Ship Integrations in Hours, Not Months"** and customer-validated copy throughout.

## Supported Connectors

- **HubSpot**: Pull sales call notes and transcripts
- **Google Drive**: Reference competitor landing pages, share output
- **Slack**: Notify your team when the landing page is ready
- **Notion**: Store customer language library and competitive intel

## Getting Started

1. Gather 3–5 recent sales call transcripts or customer interview notes
2. Identify the key outcome customers want and how they describe their current problem
3. Run the skill with your transcript source
4. Review the markdown wireframe and customer quotes used
5. Hand off to design with copy blocks ready for implementation

## Tips for Best Results

- Use calls from your ideal customer profile (ICP) — their language will resonate most
- Include both successful demos (what closed customers want) and lost deals (what didn't convince them)
- Mine exact phrases: "tired of," "need it to just work," "bleeding money on" — use real language, not paraphrases
- Include objections from calls: "but won't it break X?" → objection handler sections
- Test the headline and CTA copy against competitor landing pages
