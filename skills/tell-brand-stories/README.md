# Tell Brand Stories

> The customer is the hero, and you're here to illuminate the transformation.

## What It Does

This skill teaches you to craft emotionally resonant brand narratives by extracting real customer language, documenting your founder origin story, and building a playbook of before-after transformations that work across all channels. It includes templates for writing 30-second pitches, 2-minute explanations, and 5-minute detailed stories, plus a consistency audit to ensure your website, sales deck, support docs, and internal team all tell the same story. Includes frameworks for identifying narrative threads and positioning your company as part of a broader market trend for media pitches.

## Who It's For

- **Founders and CEOs** struggling to articulate why customers should care beyond features, trying to build emotional connection and word-of-mouth velocity

- **Marketing leaders** creating a cohesive brand voice across website, social, email, and sales materials that drives higher conversion

- **Sales leaders** equipping their teams with customer stories instead of feature lists for higher close rates and deal velocity

- **Product leaders** documenting customer transformations to align the team around what problem you're actually solving at a deep level

- **Communications and PR leads** preparing narrative angles and media pitches that aren't just product announcements but industry insights

- **Early-stage teams** where consistency matters because every hire, customer interaction, and public touchpoint represents the brand

## Use Cases

- **"Our website and sales deck tell completely different stories."** → Audit all channels, rewrite for consistency, and ensure every touchpoint references the same customer problem and transformation.

- **"Customers say 'this changed how we work' but we haven't captured that story."** → Interview 5-10 customers, extract before-after transformations with specific language, and turn them into case studies and sales collateral.

- **"Our sales team isn't closing—they're leading with features instead of outcomes."** → Build a story playbook with 3-5 detailed customer narratives and train sales to open with transformation, not technical capabilities.

- **"We're pitching journalists and getting rejected because it sounds like a product announcement."** → Develop a media narrative that positions you as part of a broader industry trend, with customer stories as supporting proof of the shift.

- **"New employees can't explain what we do in a credible way."** → Document your founder origin story in an internal handbook so every hire understands why you built this and what fundamental problem you're solving.

- **"Our customer testimonials are generic—'great product, highly recommend'—and they're not moving prospects."** → Extract specific transformations (time saved, decisions improved, confidence gained) with exact language and metrics from real customer interviews.

## Connectors

Works standalone with direct customer interviews and internal workshops, but richer with:

- **Slack** (capture customer language and wins from real conversations and feedback channels)
- **Gmail** (extract quotes and testimonial language from customer interviews and support emails)
- **Zoom** (record and transcribe customer interviews to mine authentic language)
- **Google Drive** (centralize story assets, case studies, and templates for team collaboration)
- **HubSpot** (track which stories and narratives correlate with higher conversion in your sales pipeline)

## Setup

Spend 2 hours interviewing 3-5 recent customers using this structure: "Walk me through how you were working before our product. What was hardest? What changed when you started using us? What's different now?" Record their exact language and focus on outcomes, not features. You'll have the raw material for your entire brand story framework in one focused afternoon.

## What You Get

- **Founder origin story** that explains why you built this and why you're uniquely positioned to solve the problem

- **3-5 detailed customer transformation stories** with before-and-after specifics, direct quotes, and quantified metrics

- **Brand narrative in three versions** (30-second pitch, 2-minute explanation, 5-minute detailed story) that work across different contexts

- **Consistency audit** mapping your current messaging across website, sales deck, support docs, and internal channels with recommendations

- **Customer story playbook** with case studies ready for sales, marketing, press, and investor presentations

- **Media narrative** positioning your company within a broader market trend, not just announcing a product feature release

## Key Insight

Brand stories aren't about your company—they're about the transformation your customer experiences. The best stories make the customer the hero, not you. When you anchor your narrative in real customer language and measurable outcomes, word-of-mouth and emotional connection follow naturally. Consistency across channels matters because trust builds when customers hear the same story everywhere—your website, sales call, support docs, and internal culture all reinforce the same message about what problem you solve and why it matters to people.
