# Form CRO

**Optimize any non-signup form to reduce friction, increase completion rates, and improve lead quality.**

## What This Skill Does

Forms are a conversion funnel's first moment. Every field is friction. Every confusing label costs you a lead. This skill audits your lead capture, contact, demo request, application, survey, checkout, or quote request form—and surfaces optimization opportunities grounded in how your team actually uses the data.

Harmony listens to sales calls to reveal which questions qualify leads. Support conversations show which data your team relies on. Customer discussions expose friction. This skill turns that insight into a field-by-field, layout-by-layout roadmap to increase completion rates and lead quality.

## When to Run This

- Your form completion rate is flat or declining
- Sales team complains about incomplete or low-quality submissions
- Mobile abandonment is significantly higher than desktop
- Multi-step form abandonment spikes at certain steps
- You're redesigning a form and want data-driven priorities
- Checkout or application form has high bounce rates
- You're adding new fields and want to know which ones matter

## How It Works

The skill walks through a structured audit:

1. **Current state** — Map every field, type, and drop-off point
2. **Data usage** — Trace where each field flows; identify vanity fields
3. **Drop-off patterns** — Spot where prospects abandon the form
4. **Label clarity** — Assess jargon, friction, and benefit framing
5. **Layout & mobile** — Identify single-column opportunities and mobile breaks
6. **Trust signals** — Check for progress bars, explanations, privacy links
7. **Recommendations** — Prioritize changes; create an A/B test roadmap

Output includes field-by-field audit, layout redesign, and 4+ A/B test ideas with measurement approach.

## Connectors Used

- **HubSpot** — Form completion rates, field-level drop-off, lead quality metrics
- **Slack** — Sales and support feedback on form quality and friction
- **Gmail** — Follow-up email patterns showing which data is actually used
- **Google Drive** — Form designs, wireframes, and test documentation

## Example Output

The skill provides a complete audit of a demo request form, including:
- Current completion rate (32%) and primary drop-off (60% at Company Size field)
- Field-by-field assessment with recommendations
- 2-step form redesign to reduce cognitive load
- 4 A/B tests (single-page vs. multi-step, label clarity, conditional fields, trust signals)
- Measurement approach to validate improvements

## Quick Start

1. Gather your form (screenshot or URL)
2. Run the skill
3. Review the field-by-field audit
4. Pick the highest-impact test from the roadmap
5. Implement, run for 2 weeks, measure completion rate and lead quality

---

Form optimization is iterative. Small wins compound. Start with field removal and label clarity, then test layout and trust signals. Harmony data—sales calls, support feedback, email patterns—grounds every recommendation in how your team actually works.
