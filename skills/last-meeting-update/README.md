# Last Meeting Catch-Up

> Your personal assistant who remembers every conversation — get a 30-second brief instead of digging through notes.

## What It Does

Ever find yourself about to call someone and can't quite remember what was discussed last time? Or get pulled into back-to-back meetings and lose track of what happened in earlier conversations? Last Meeting Catch-Up cuts through the noise.

In seconds, you get a conversational summary of your most recent meeting with any contact, team, or organization. Think of it as a colleague leaning over and saying, "Here's what happened with them last time—here's what they're waiting on from you, here's what you need to know before you reach out again."

The skill delivers:
- **What was discussed** — the actual topics and direction of the conversation
- **Key decisions** — what you both agreed on
- **Open items** — what's still being figured out
- **Action items** — yours and theirs, with due dates if mentioned
- **Tone and energy** — was this productive? Tense? A positive relationship?
- **Time-sensitive flags** — anything that needs your attention before the next conversation
- **A conversation starter** — exactly what to say next time you reach out, so you show them you were actually listening

## Who It's For

**For Everyone.** Whether you're in sales, customer success, product management, operations, or leadership:
- Sales reps juggling 20 active deals and 50+ contacts
- Account managers balancing multiple client relationships
- Customer success teams rotating coverage
- Product managers collaborating across teams
- Executives preparing for calls with stakeholders or board members
- Anyone who's ever forgotten what was promised, decided, or discussed in their last meeting

## Use Cases

**Before a Follow-Up Call**
You're about to reach out to a prospect. Instead of opening 3 different documents to find where you left off, ask Last Meeting Catch-Up for a brief. Now you sound prepared, attentive, and professional—because you remember exactly where the conversation ended.

**After a Busy Day**
Your calendar was insane. You had a call with a key contact but didn't have time to process it. Get up to speed in 30 seconds so you can action items before the day ends.

**Remembering Commitments**
"Didn't I promise to send something to Sarah?" You did—and Catch-Up reminds you before you forget (or worse, before she follows up asking where it is).

**Understanding Where Things Stand**
Your deal stalled, your customer's gone quiet, or a project feels stuck. What actually happened last time you talked? What's blocking progress? Catch-Up gives you the real picture without the time investment.

**Team Handoffs**
Someone's covering for you while you're out. They need to know what was discussed with your biggest accounts. Catch-Up gives them the context they need to have a smart conversation on your behalf.

**Preparing for Strategic Conversations**
You're meeting with a senior stakeholder next week. What did you talk about last time? What commitments are still pending? What were their top priorities? Walk in ready and credible.

## Setup

No special setup required. Last Meeting Catch-Up works with your existing conversation history—meeting transcripts, call notes, calendar entries, and recorded meetings all feed into the brief.

Just ask: "Update me on my last meeting with [contact]" or "What did we talk about with [company]?"
