# AI Search Optimization (AEO) Analyzer

> **Stop ranking for Google alone.** Optimize your content for AI search engines—ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews—where answers appear *before* links.

---

## What It Does

AI search engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity don't rank websites; they synthesize and cite content from across the web to answer user questions. **AI Search Optimization (AEO)** is about making your content so clear, authoritative, and well-structured that AI models naturally pull your insights into the answers users see first.

This skill audits your current presence in AI-generated responses, identifies why competitors' content ranks higher, and recommends concrete optimizations to increase citations without requiring a Google rank.

It works by:
1. **Mapping your AI visibility** across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Claude
2. **Analyzing content extractability** (Can AI models pull clean, citable passages?)
3. **Evaluating authority signals** (Are you cited as an expert source?)
4. **Recommending structural optimizations** (Definition blocks, comparison tables, FAQ sections)
5. **Setting up citation monitoring** (Track which content appears in AI responses over time)

## Setup

1. **Connect at least two data sources** (Google Drive + Slack recommended) to maximize context and cross-functional insights.
2. **Identify your top 8–12 content pieces** (blog posts, guides, case studies) to audit first.
3. **Run initial searches** in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google Search (look for AI Overviews) to establish baseline visibility.
4. **Schedule 2–3 hours** for full audit (visibility mapping + extractability analysis + optimization roadmap).
