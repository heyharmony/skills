# Challenger Sale Scorecard

> Evaluate your reps using the methodology that wins in 60% of deals — teach unique perspective, tailor around insight, take control of the process.

## What It Does

Scores sales calls using the Challenger Sale framework: teaching a unique perspective, tailoring around the prospect's specific situation, and taking control of the sales process. Extracts moments from Harmony call recordings or transcripts, identifies where reps excelled and missed opportunities, and provides specific coaching tied to each pillar. Works without Harmony data using call summaries and coaching questions. The output is a scorecard that shows patterns over time and concrete coaching steps.

## Who It's For

- **Sales managers** coaching reps with a proven methodology instead of gut feel
- **Sales enablement leaders** measuring rep quality and identifying training gaps systematically
- **VP Sales** tracking whether rep behavior correlates with deal outcomes
- **Reps** themselves wanting to understand how they're executing Challenger methodology
- **Founders** evaluating whether sales velocity improves when reps adopt Challenger behaviors

## Use Cases

- Weekly rep coaching; want to give feedback based on Challenger behaviors, not just deal outcome → Call scorecard with specific moments and coaching
- Rep is struggling; need to diagnose if it's teaching, tailoring, or control → Separate scores show which pillar needs work
- Quarterly rep assessment; track progress across 10+ calls → Show which reps are adopting Challenger behaviors and which are stuck
- New rep training; use scored calls from top performers as training material → Show examples of strong teach, tailor, control moments
- Sales leader wants to prove to the board that rep quality drives outcomes → Correlate Challenger scores with close rate and deal size

## Connectors

Works standalone, but richer with these integrations:

- **Salesforce / HubSpot** — Pulls call recordings, transcripts, opportunity stage, and competitor mentions to show how Challenger behaviors correlate with deal progression
- **Slack** — Pulls team discussions about competitive moves, customer insights, and market trends that reps should be teaching about
- **Google Drive** — References customer case studies, industry research, and competitive data that informs what reps should teach

## Setup

Install and go. Works best when call recordings or transcripts live in Harmony — the skill analyzes what was actually said. Without Harmony, provide a call summary (key moments, objections, rep responses) and the skill will score and coach based on that.

## What You Get

A detailed Challenger Sale scorecard for each call showing: three pillar scores (Teach, Tailor, Control), overall Challenger score, specific coaching moments with timestamps and what to do differently, root cause analysis, and rep progress trends across recent calls. Includes next coaching steps and measurement strategy to track improvement.
