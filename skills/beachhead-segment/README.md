# Beachhead Market Finder

> Stop trying to sell to everyone. Find the one segment you can dominate first — then expand from a position of strength.

## What It Does

Evaluates your potential market segments against four criteria — burning pain, willingness to pay, winnability, and referral potential — and recommends the single best beachhead to pursue. When customer conversations live in Harmony, the scoring is grounded in real pain signals, buying urgency, and competitive mentions from actual discovery calls, not assumptions.

## Who It's For

- **Founders** launching a product and choosing where to start
- **Product leaders** refocusing after trying to serve too many segments
- **GTM teams** aligning on who to target first with limited resources
- **Investors / advisors** helping portfolio companies sharpen their market entry

## Use Cases

- Built a product, not sure who to sell to first → Scored segment analysis with a clear #1 recommendation and validation plan
- Selling to 5 different types of customers, none at scale → Retroactive beachhead analysis based on existing customer data
- Entering a new market or geography → Segment evaluation with expansion path mapping
- Post-pivot, need to pick a new starting point → Fresh segment scoring with conversation evidence from new discovery calls

## Setup

Install and go. For the strongest analysis, have customer discovery calls in Harmony — the skill uses pain language, buying signals, and referral patterns from real conversations to ground the segment scoring.

## What You Get

A beachhead market analysis with: single recommended segment with rationale, scored comparison of 5–8 candidate segments, deep-dive on the winner (archetype, buying triggers, decision process, voice of customer), clear "why not" reasoning for runner-up segments, staged expansion path from beachhead to broader market, and a validation plan with specific tests and success signals.
