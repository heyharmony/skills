# SEO Audit

> Uncover ranking blockers, technical debt, and content gaps in minutes. Diagnose why traffic dropped, why you're not ranking, and what to fix first.

## What It Does

This skill conducts a systematic audit across crawlability, technical foundations, on-page optimization, content quality, and internal linking. It surfaces actionable issues ranked by impact—from critical indexation blockers to quick-win meta description updates—with specific fixes, effort estimates, and an implementation roadmap.

Use it to baseline your site's SEO health, diagnose sudden traffic drops, understand why target keywords aren't ranking, or prepare for a content/redesign overhaul.

## Who It's For

- **Marketing managers** — Need a clear picture of SEO blockers before pitching roadmaps to product/dev
- **Content leads** — Want to audit content depth, E-E-A-T signals, and topic coverage vs. competitors
- **Product/Engineering teams** — Need to understand technical SEO impact of redesigns, migrations, or architectural changes
- **SEO specialists** — Running quarterly health checks or post-campaign diagnostics
- **Growth teams** — Diagnosing traffic declines or identifying ranking opportunity gaps
- **Founders/CMOs** — Baseline pre-launch or monthly checkpoint against competitive landscape

## Use Cases

- **Traffic drop diagnosis** — A spike in crawl errors, Core Web Vitals failure, or broken redirects may explain a 30% traffic decline
- **Ranking audit** — Identify keyword cannibalization, thin content, missing author bios, or poor internal link structure blocking rank improvements
- **Pre-launch SEO review** — Catch crawlability, indexation, and on-page issues before going live to avoid day-one visibility loss
- **Competitor gap analysis** — Compare your on-page, technical, and content foundations against top-ranking competitors
- **Content strategy planning** — Baseline current topic coverage and identify long-tail/cluster gaps to prioritize writing
- **Migration/redesign validation** — Verify no high-traffic pages were lost, redirects work correctly, and Core Web Vitals recovered post-launch

## Connectors

This skill integrates with:

- **Google Drive** — Pull in existing SEO docs, keyword research, competitor analyses, audit templates
- **Slack** — Reference team discussions about traffic issues, ranking losses, site changes, and remediation plans
- **HubSpot** — Correlate page performance metrics with MQL/SQL conversion by landing page, identify high-intent pages underperforming

(Can run standalone without connectors, but data pulls accelerate diagnosis.)

## Setup

1. **Gather inputs.** Know your domain, primary target keywords, business goals (leads, trials, sales), and any recent traffic/ranking changes.
2. **Access tools** (optional but recommended):
   - Google Search Console (crawl errors, indexation, mobile usability, Core Web Vitals)
   - Google Analytics 4 (traffic patterns, user behavior by page)
   - SEO crawlers: Screaming Frog, Semrush, Ahrefs, or Moz (comprehensive crawl + competitive benchmarking)
3. **Run the audit** using the 10-step instruction framework.
4. **Output** is a ranked dashboard of issues, fixes, effort, and implementation timeline.

## What You Get

- **Executive summary** — Health score (0–100), critical/high/medium/low issue counts, traffic impact estimate, recommended timeline
- **Ranked issue breakdown** by category:
  - Crawlability & indexation (robots.txt, sitemaps, 404s, canonicals)
  - Technical foundations (Core Web Vitals, mobile, HTTPS, server health)
  - On-page optimization (titles, meta descriptions, headings, alt text)
  - Content quality & E-E-A-T (depth, author bios, case studies, freshness)
  - Internal linking (density, anchor text, orphans, clusters)
  - Keyword strategy (cannibalization, intent alignment, long-tail gaps)
- **For each issue:** Problem statement, affected page count/examples, business impact, specific fix, effort (days), and success metric
- **Implementation roadmap** — Phased timeline (typically 6–8 weeks) with owner assignments and validation checkpoints
- **Next steps** — Recommended audit cadence and trigger-based monitoring

Perfect for monthly status updates, quarterly reviews, or post-launch validation.
