# North Star Metric

> Find the one metric that tells you whether you're winning — and the levers that move it.

## What It Does

Classifies your business game (Attention, Transaction, or Productivity), identifies a single North Star Metric validated against 7 criteria, and defines 3-5 Input Metrics that form your metrics constellation. Stress-tests the choice against Goodhart's Law and alternative candidates so you ship a metric framework you can trust.

## Who It's For

Product leaders, founders, and growth teams who need a clear metrics framework — whether you're choosing your first North Star or pressure-testing an existing one.

## Use Cases

- Setting up a metrics framework for a new product → get a validated NSM and input metrics with ownership mapped to teams
- Preparing for a planning cycle → structured metrics constellation that aligns product, marketing, and CS around one outcome
- After a round of customer interviews → NSM grounded in what users actually say they value, not internal assumptions

## Setup

Install and go. Provide your business context: what you do, who you serve, your business model, and current stage. For richer results, include your company vision, current metrics, and point to Harmony conversations where customers describe the value they get.

## What You Get

A complete metrics framework: business game classification, a validated North Star Metric with criteria scorecard, 3-5 input metrics with definitions and team ownership, a constellation map, stress-test results, and recommended next steps.
